My family usually shops at the open air market. It offers a wide range of products. We can find fresh home-made and home-grown products here, which are typically hard to find in supermarkets. Also, we can bargain for lower prices. It is fun to bargain and buy things at lower prices. On the other hand, we know the sellers at those markets very well and we always enjoy chatting with them.
