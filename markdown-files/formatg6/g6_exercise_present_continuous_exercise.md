### Công thức

**(+) Chủ từ + _______ + _______ + ….**

**Ví dụ:** 
- I _______ learning English. 
- He _______ learning English. 
- They _______ learning English.

---

**(-)** **Chủ từ + _______ + _______ + V-ing + ….**

**Lưu ý:**
- are not = _______
- is not = _______

## HƯỚNG DẪN ĐIỀN

**Các từ cần điền:**
- am/is/are
- V-ing
- not
- aren't
- isn't
- now
- at the moment
- at present

---

### PHẦN TRẮC NGHIỆM THÊM

**Điền am/is/are:**

1. I _____ reading a book now.
2. She _____ cooking dinner at the moment.
3. They _____ playing soccer right now.
4. He _____ writing a letter at present.
5. We _____ studying English now.

**Điền V-ing:**

6. Look! The children are _____ (run) in the park.
7. She is _____ (sing) a beautiful song now.
8. I am _____ (do) my homework at the moment.
9. They are _____ (watch) TV right now.
10. He is _____ (eat) lunch at present.
