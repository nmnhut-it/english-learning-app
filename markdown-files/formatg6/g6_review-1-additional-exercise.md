**GHI CHÚ CHO GIÁO VIÊN:**

**Điểm ngữ pháp được luyện tập:**
- Present Simple & Present Continuous
- There is/There are structures
- Possessive forms ('s, s')
- Prepositions of place
- Question formation
- Verb to be & Have/Has

**Từ vựng theo chủ đề:**
- School subjects and activities
- House and rooms
- Family and friends
- Daily routines
- Descriptions (appearance and personality)
