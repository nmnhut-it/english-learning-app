**UNIT 5: FOOD AND DRINK**

**COMMUNICATION** 

**Hỏi giá:** 

How much \+is/are \+ món hàng? 

It is/they are \+ giá tiền \+ đơn vị 

Ví dụ: How much are five apples? → They are 20000 Viet Nam Dong. 

**Bài 2 trang 55** 

**Vocabulary**

   → It is 20 000 dong

   → It is 8 000 dong

   → It is 5 000 dong
