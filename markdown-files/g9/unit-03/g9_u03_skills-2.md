**Bài mẫu**

There are many things we should do to manage our time effectively. Firstly, making a plan or schedule for things you need to do, like appointments, projects, homework, and tests, can help you stay organized. Secondly, giving priority to the most important task helps you concentrate on what needs to be done first. Lastly, building an effective daily routine can help you accomplish your tasks more efficiently. Using tools like a calendar, a diary, or a mobile app can also help you remember what you need to do and when you need to do it. By following these tips, you can manage your time better and reduce stress.
