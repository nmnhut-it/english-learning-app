**ACROSS (NGANG):**

3. (adj) connected with the place you are living (Brochure b) = **LOCAL**

**DOWN (DỌC):**
2. (n) the way of life of a group of people or countries (Brochure b) = **CULTURE**

### Bài 3 (trang 114)

**3. Which tour does each statement below talk about? Write a, b, c or d.**

*(Mỗi câu dưới đây nói về chuyến tham quan nào? Viết a, b, c hoặc d.)*

1. You can buy arts and crafts to help protect the environment.
2. You can learn how to help protect wildlife.
3. This tour is not suitable for families with children.
4. This tour offers educational videos about the environment.

#### Lời giải chi tiết:

| Câu | Đáp án | Giải thích |
|-----|--------|------------|
| 1 | d | "Buy local souvenirs to help the local Whale Protection Program" |
| 2 | c | "Learn how you can help save wild animals in the local research centre" |
| 3 | b | "Price: $80/adult (children not allowed)" |
| 4 | a | "Watch 3D documentaries to learn about sea animals and the coral reef" |

#### Hướng dẫn dịch:

1. Bạn có thể mua các tác phẩm nghệ thuật và thủ công để giúp bảo vệ môi trường. → **d (Hawaii)**
2. Bạn có thể học cách giúp bảo vệ động vật hoang dã. → **c (Zimbabwe)**
3. Tour này không thích hợp cho gia đình có trẻ em. → **b (Sapa)**
4. Chuyến tham quan này cung cấp các video giáo dục về môi trường. → **a (Australia)**

### Bài 4 (trang 114)

**4. Work in groups. Think of new ideas for making one of the tours better for the environment**

*(Làm việc nhóm. Nghĩ ra những ý tưởng mới để thực hiện một trong những chuyến tham quan tốt hơn cho môi trường)*

#### Lời giải mẫu:

**A. Great Barrier Reef Tour (Australia):**
- If they ban swimming with fish, the Great Barrier Reef will be better protected.
- Tourists should not be allowed to dive very close to the coral reefs.
- Use environmentally-friendly sunscreen that doesn't harm the coral.

**B. Sapa Trekking Tour (Viet Nam):**
- Tourists should always follow the walking paths and trails.
- The local people with whom the tourists stay should only use local ingredients.
- If they only use local ingredients, their carbon footprint will be lower.

**C. Zimbabwe's National Park Tour:**
- If tourists choose electric cars instead of jeeps that run on fuel, they will be more eco-friendly.
- Limit the number of vehicles allowed in the park each day.

**D. Whale-watching Tour (Hawaii):**
- The boats should not get too close to the whales or make too much noise.
- If the boats are environmentally friendly, they will not harm the whales or their habitats.
- Use boats that use less fuel and oil, make small waves and are quiet.
