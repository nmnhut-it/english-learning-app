1. biodiversity

a. the variety of plants and animals in a particular area
b. animals and plants that grow in natural conditions
c. changes in the world's weather, especially an increase in temperature
d. the natural environment in which a plant or an animal lives
e. all the plants and animals in an area and the way they affect each other and the environment

<answer>
**Đáp án:** 1-a | 2-d | 3-e | 4-b | 5-c

<explanation>

**5-c. climate change** - "biến đổi khí hậu" = thay đổi thời tiết trên thế giới, đặc biệt nhiệt độ tăng.
</explanation>

### Grammar - Câu tường thuật (Reported Speech)

<teacher_script pause="0">
Phần ngữ pháp: <eng>Reported speech</eng> - Câu tường thuật. Đây là điểm ngữ pháp chính Unit 9.
</teacher_script>

<grammar>
**Câu tường thuật (Reported speech):**

Khi thuật lại lời ai đó nói, ta cần:
1. Đổi đại từ
2. Lùi thì (tense shift)
3. Đổi trạng từ thời gian/nơi chốn

**Quy tắc lùi thì:**
- Present Simple → Past Simple
- Present Continuous → Past Continuous
- Present Perfect → Past Perfect
- will → would | can → could | must → had to

**Đổi trạng từ:**
- today → that day | tomorrow → the next/following day
- yesterday → the day before | next week → the following week
- here → there | this → that

**Câu tường thuật với câu hỏi:**
- Wh-question: "Where do you live?" → He asked me **where I lived**.
- Yes/No question: "Do you like it?" → He asked me **if I liked** it.
</grammar>

<task>
**Đề:** Choose the correct word or phrase to complete each sentence.
**Dịch đề:** Chọn từ hoặc cụm từ đúng để hoàn thành câu.

<questions type="multiple_choice">
**1.** Minh's teacher asked him if he **was / is** ready to present the following day.
**2.** Nam's father **suggested / denied** that Nam should focus on one aspect of the problem.
**3.** Tuan said he would complete his project **the following week / next week**.
**4.** Mai **asked / said** her teacher where she could get the information form.
**5.** Phong said he **handed / had handed** in his project the previous day.
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Ok đáp án nè.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Đáp án:** 1. was | 2. suggested | 3. the following week | 4. asked | 5. had handed
</answer>

<explanation>
**1. was** - Câu tường thuật lùi thì: "is" → "was". Cô hỏi liệu Minh **đã** sẵn sàng chưa.
**2. suggested** - "Deny" = phủ nhận, không hợp nghĩa. "Suggest" = gợi ý, hợp với "should focus".
**3. the following week** - Trong câu gián tiếp, "next week" → "the following week".
**4. asked** - Câu hỏi gián tiếp (where she could get) dùng "asked", không dùng "said".
**5. had handed** - Câu gián tiếp lùi thì: "handed" (quá khứ đơn) → "had handed" (quá khứ hoàn thành).
</explanation>

### Grammar - Bài 2 trang 99

<teacher_script pause="60">
Bài 2, chuyển các câu trực tiếp sang câu tường thuật nha. Nhớ lùi thì, đổi đại từ, và đổi trạng từ! 1 phút.
</teacher_script>

<task>
**Đề:** Change these sentences into reported speech.
**Dịch đề:** Chuyển câu trực tiếp sang câu tường thuật.
</task>

<questions type="complete_sentences">
**1.** "The burning of coal leads to air pollution," Mrs Le explained.
**2.** "I have to present my paper on endangered animals next week," Nam said.
**3.** "Do human activities have an impact on the environment?" Linda asked the speaker.
**4.** "What environmental projects is your school working on?" Nam asked Mai.
**5.** "I will read more articles before writing the essay, Nam," Tom said.
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Ok đáp án nè.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Đáp án:**
1. Mrs Le explained that the burning of coal led/leads to air pollution.
2. Nam said he had to present his paper on endangered animals the following week.
3. Linda asked the speaker if/whether human activities had/have an impact on the environment.
4. Nam asked Mai what environmental projects her school was working on.
5. Tom said to/told Nam that he would read more articles before writing the essay.
</answer>

<explanation>
**Câu 1:** "leads" (hiện tại đơn) → "led" (quá khứ đơn). Sự thật khoa học có thể giữ nguyên thì.
**Câu 2:** "have to" → "had to". "next week" → "the following week". "I" → "he".
**Câu 3:** Câu hỏi Yes/No → dùng "if/whether". "have" → "had". "Do human activities" → word order thay đổi.
**Câu 4:** Câu hỏi Wh- → "what environmental projects her school was working on" (không đảo ngữ).
**Câu 5:** "will read" → "would read". "I" → "he". Xóa "Nam" vì đã có trong "said to Nam".
</explanation>
