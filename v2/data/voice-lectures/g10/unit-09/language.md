### Pronunciation - Rhythm

<teacher_script pause="0">

</teacher_script>

<grammar>
**Nhịp điệu câu (Rhythm):**
- Các từ nội dung (content words) được nhấn mạnh
- Các từ chức năng (function words) không nhấn
- Khoảng cách giữa các từ nhấn gần bằng nhau

Ví dụ:
- **Don't pick** the **flowers** when you **go** to the **park**.
- The **students clean** the **school playground every weekend**.
- **What's happening** with the **polar bears**?
</grammar>

### Grammar - Câu tường thuật

<teacher_script pause="0">
Phần ngữ pháp: <eng>Reported speech</eng> - Câu tường thuật. Đây là điểm ngữ pháp chính Unit 9.
</teacher_script>

<grammar>
**Câu tường thuật (Reported speech):**

Khi thuật lại lời ai đó nói, ta cần:
1. Đổi đại từ
2. Lùi thì (tense shift)
3. Đổi trạng từ thời gian/nơi chốn

**Công thức:**
- Direct: He **said**, "I **am** studying."
- Reported: He **said** (that) he **was** studying.

**Quy tắc lùi thì:**
- Present Simple → Past Simple
- Present Continuous → Past Continuous
- Present Perfect → Past Perfect
- will → would
- can → could
- must → had to

**Đổi trạng từ:**
- today → that day
- tomorrow → the next day / the following day
- yesterday → the day before / the previous day
- next week → the following week
- here → there
- this → that

**Câu tường thuật với advise/suggest:**
- She **advised** that I **should** start with small actions.
- She **suggested** that we **should** focus on each problem.
</grammar>

### Grammar - Bài 1 trang 98

<teacher_script pause="60">

<task>
**Đề:** Rewrite the following sentences in reported speech.
**Dịch đề:** Viết lại câu sang câu tường thuật.
</task>

<questions type="complete_sentences">
**1.** "I am doing my environmental project," Nam said.
**2.** "We will present our project next week," the students said.
**3.** "I can help you with your presentation," Mai said to Nam.
**4.** "You should start with small actions," the teacher advised.
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Ok đáp án nè.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Đáp án:**
1. Nam said (that) he was doing his environmental project.
2. The students said (that) they would present their project the following week.
3. Mai said to Nam (that) she could help him with his presentation.
4. The teacher advised (that) he/they should start with small actions.
</answer>

<explanation>
**Câu 1:** "am doing" → "was doing" (Present Continuous → Past Continuous). "I" → "he".
**Câu 2:** "will present" → "would present". "We" → "they". "next week" → "the following week".
**Câu 3:** "can help" → "could help". "I" → "she". "you" → "him". "your" → "his".
**Câu 4:** Dùng "advised" nên giữ "should" không đổi.
</explanation>
