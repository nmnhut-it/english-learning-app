### Grammar - So sánh hơn và so sánh nhất

<teacher_script pause="0">
Phần ngữ pháp: <eng>Comparative and superlative adjectives</eng> - So sánh hơn và so sánh nhất.
</teacher_script>

<grammar>
**So sánh hơn (Comparative):**
- Tính từ ngắn: adj + -er + than
  - Ví dụ: easier than, larger than
- Tính từ dài: more + adj + than
  - Ví dụ: more attractive than, more competitive than
- Bất quy tắc: good → better, bad → worse

**So sánh nhất (Superlative):**
- Tính từ ngắn: the + adj + -est
  - Ví dụ: the easiest, the largest
- Tính từ dài: the most + adj
  - Ví dụ: the most popular, the most attractive
- Bất quy tắc: good → the best, bad → the worst

**So sánh kém hơn:** less + adj + than
- Ví dụ: less competitive than (kém cạnh tranh hơn)
</grammar>

<questions type="multiple_choice">
**1.** WTO rules make trade **the easiest / easier** for smaller member countries.
**2.** This job offer is **more attractive / the most attractive** than the previous one.
**3.** Because of poorly designed packaging, our products are **less competitive / the least competitive** than foreign products.
**4.** Over the past few years, Viet Nam has become one of **more popular / the most popular** destinations for foreign visitors in Southeast Asia.
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Ok đáp án nè.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Đáp án:** 1. easier | 2. more attractive | 3. less competitive | 4. the most popular
</answer>

<explanation>
**1. easier** - So sánh hơn vì so sánh thương mại trước và sau khi có WTO. Không có "the" nên không phải so sánh nhất.

**2. more attractive** - So sánh hơn vì có "than the previous one" - so sánh 2 lời mời làm việc.

**3. less competitive** - So sánh kém hơn vì có "than foreign products" - sản phẩm nội kém cạnh tranh hơn sản phẩm ngoại.

**4. the most popular** - So sánh nhất vì có "one of" (một trong những) + the most + adj. Việt Nam là một trong những điểm đến được yêu thích nhất.
</explanation>

<questions type="complete_sentences">
**1.** No place is more popular with foreign visitors than this city. (the most popular)
**2.** In many supermarkets, imported goods are not as expensive as locally produced goods. (cheaper)
**3.** No international organisation is larger than the United Nations. (the largest)
**4.** In the past, our country wasn't as active on the international stage as it is today. (more active)
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Ok đáp án nè.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Đáp án:**
1. This city is the most popular (place) with foreign visitors.
2. In many supermarkets, imported goods are cheaper than locally produced goods.
3. The United Nations is the largest international organisation.
4. Today, our country is more active on the international stage than it was in the past.
</answer>

<explanation>
**Câu 1:** "No place is more popular than X" = "X is the most popular" - Không nơi nào phổ biến hơn = X phổ biến nhất.

**Câu 2:** "not as expensive as" = "cheaper than" - Hàng nhập không đắt bằng = hàng nhập rẻ hơn.

**Câu 3:** "No organisation is larger than X" = "X is the largest" - Không tổ chức nào lớn hơn = X lớn nhất.

**Câu 4:** "wasn't as active as today" = "today is more active than past" - Đảo ngược thời gian so sánh.
</explanation>
