<questions type="fill_blanks">
As a member of various international organisations, Viet Nam can (1) _______ into relations with other countries. Our country (2) _______ to work closely with these organisations.

As Viet Nam (3) _______ to develop its economy, it (4) _______ foreign investors. This will help (5) _______ our economic growth.
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Ok đáp án nè.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Đáp án:** 1. enter | 2. commits | 3. aims | 4. welcomes | 5. promote
</answer>

<explanation>
**1. enter** - Việt Nam có thể "bước vào" quan hệ với các nước. Sau "can" dùng V nguyên mẫu.
**2. commits** - Nước ta "cam kết" hợp tác. Chủ ngữ "Our country" số ít nên thêm "s".
**3. aims** - Việt Nam "nhằm mục đích" phát triển kinh tế. Chủ ngữ số ít.
**4. welcomes** - Việt Nam "chào đón" nhà đầu tư nước ngoài. Chủ ngữ số ít.
**5. promote** - Giúp "thúc đẩy" tăng trưởng kinh tế. Sau "help" dùng V nguyên mẫu.
</explanation>

<questions type="complete_sentences">
**1.** Viet Nam wasn't as attractive to foreign tourists as it is nowadays. (more attractive)
**2.** The United Nations is the largest international organisation. (larger)
**3.** The international market wasn't as competitive as it is now. (more competitive)
**4.** No other country is a bigger financial provider to this organisation than Japan. (the biggest)
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Ok đáp án nè.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Đáp án:**
1. Viet Nam is more attractive to foreign tourists nowadays than it was.
2. No other international organisation is larger than the United Nations.
3. The international market is more competitive now than it was.
4. Japan is the biggest financial provider to this organisation.
</answer>

<explanation>
**Câu 1:** "wasn't as attractive as nowadays" => "is more attractive nowadays than it was" - Đổi từ "không... bằng" sang "hơn".

**Câu 2:** "X is the largest" => "No other... is larger than X" - Đổi từ so sánh nhất sang so sánh hơn với "No other".

**Câu 3:** Tương tự câu 1, đổi "not as... as" sang "more... than".

**Câu 4:** "No other country is bigger than Japan" => "Japan is the biggest" - Đổi từ so sánh hơn với "No other" sang so sánh nhất.
</explanation>
