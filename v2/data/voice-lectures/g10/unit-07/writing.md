<questions type="fill_blanks">
cultural | open to | popular | experiencing | easier | international

**1.** Our country has become one of the most _______ destinations for foreign tourists in the region.
**2.** Various _______ exchanges help visitors know more about our country.
**3.** Greater educational opportunities abroad are now _______ Vietnamese students.
**4.** It is also _______ for Vietnamese people to travel abroad.
**5.** Viet Nam's colleges and universities can also accept _______ students.
**6.** Vietnamese people have a better chance of _______ different cultures.
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Ok đáp án nè.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Đáp án:** 1. popular | 2. cultural | 3. open to | 4. easier | 5. international | 6. experiencing
</answer>

<explanation>
**1. popular** - Điểm đến "phổ biến nhất" (the most popular destinations).
**2. cultural** - Giao lưu "văn hóa" (cultural exchanges) giúp du khách biết thêm về nước ta.
**3. open to** - Cơ hội giáo dục "mở ra cho" (open to) sinh viên Việt Nam.
**4. easier** - "Dễ dàng hơn" (easier) cho người Việt đi du lịch nước ngoài.
**5. international** - Trường đại học có thể tiếp nhận sinh viên "quốc tế" (international).
**6. experiencing** - Cơ hội "trải nghiệm" (experiencing) các nền văn hóa khác nhau.
</explanation>

### Bài 3 trang 80 - Writing

<teacher_script pause="300">
Bài 3, viết đoạn văn 120 đến 150 từ về lợi ích khi Việt Nam là thành viên của các tổ chức quốc tế. Có 3 lợi ích chính: quảng bá văn hóa, tạo cơ hội giáo dục, và tăng du lịch. 5 phút nha.
</teacher_script>

<task>
**Đề:** Write a paragraph (120-150 words) about the benefits for Viet Nam as a member of international organisations.
**Dịch đề:** Viết đoạn văn (120-150 từ) về lợi ích khi Việt Nam là thành viên của các tổ chức quốc tế.
</task>

<answer>
**Bài mẫu:**
Viet Nam has gained three main benefits since it joined different international organisations. First, it can promote its culture and get to know other cultures. Various cultural exchanges help visitors know more about our country. Besides, Vietnamese people have a better chance of experiencing different cultures. Second, joining international organisations helps create more educational opportunities for both Vietnamese and foreign students. Greater educational opportunities abroad are now open to Vietnamese students. Viet Nam's colleges and universities can also accept international students. Finally, both local and international tours have increased. Our country has become one of the most popular destinations for foreign tourists in the region. It is also easier for Vietnamese people to travel abroad.
</answer>
