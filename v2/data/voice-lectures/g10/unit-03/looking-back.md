### Grammar 1 - Compound Sentences

<teacher_script pause="60">

<questions type="matching">
**1.** She writes her own songs, → ___
**2.** He participated in many talent competitions, → ___
**3.** We can go to a live concert, → ___
**4.** The traffic was really bad, → ___

a. or we can stay at home to watch Vietnam Idol.
b. so we decided to walk to the stadium.
c. and they always have deep meanings.
d. but the judges never liked his songs.
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="0" href="audio/tts_29c83a10.mp3">
Ok đáp án nè.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Đáp án:** 1-c | 2-d | 3-a | 4-b
</answer>

<explanation>
**1-c:** and = thêm thông tin (viết bài hát VÀ chúng có ý nghĩa sâu)
**2-d:** but = tương phản (tham gia cuộc thi NHƯNG giám khảo không thích)
**3-a:** or = lựa chọn (đi concert HOẶC ở nhà)
**4-b:** so = kết quả (giao thông xấu NÊN đi bộ)
</explanation>

### Grammar 2 - To-infinitive / Bare infinitive

<teacher_script pause="60">
Phần ngữ pháp 2: Tìm và sửa lỗi sai nha. 1 phút.
</teacher_script>

<task>
**Đề:** Find the mistake and correct it.
**Dịch đề:** Tìm lỗi sai và sửa.
</task>

<questions type="fill_blanks">
**1.** She asked him attend the school performance.
**2.** Don't let her to go to the music festival!
**3.** Their parents will never agree buy that expensive piano.
**4.** Her father made her to sing at family gatherings.
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="0" href="audio/tts_29c83a10.mp3">
Ok đáp án nè.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Đáp án:**
1. attend → to attend (ask + O + to V)
2. to go → go (let + O + V)
3. buy → to buy (agree + to V)
4. to sing → sing (make + O + V)
</answer>

<explanation>
Nhớ nha:
- **ask, want, agree, decide, plan** + to V (có to)
- **let, make, hear, notice** + V (không to)
</explanation>
