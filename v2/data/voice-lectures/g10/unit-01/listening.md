### Bài 3 trang 15 - True/False

<teacher_script pause="60">
Bài 3 <eng>Listen to the talk show and decide whether the statements are true or false</eng>. Nghe rồi xem câu nào đúng câu nào sai nha. 1 phút.
</teacher_script>

<task>
**Đề:** Listen to the talk show and decide whether the statements are true (T) or false (F).
**Dịch đề:** Nghe chương trình và quyết định những câu sau đúng hay sai.
</task>

<reading>
**Tapescript:**

**Hieu:** Good evening. Well, I live with my parents and my brother. And I must say that all my achievements are the result of their support.

**Host:** Do your parents help you with your studies?

**Hieu:** My parents are farmers. They know little about physics. But they believe in me. They always listen to me and cheer me up whenever I have difficulties.

**Host:** So they encourage you to try harder?

**Hieu:** That's right. Their love and trust give me strength to carry on.

**Host:** How about your brother?

**Hieu:** My brother is my best friend. He shares his happy and sad moments with me, and is always there for me when I need help.

**Host:** You're so lucky to have a supportive family. Do you spend a lot of time together?

**Hieu:** Yes. My parents have created some family routines so that every week we can spend some time together although we're all very busy.
</reading>

<questions type="true_false">
**1.** There are three people in Hieu's family.
*Có ba người trong gia đình Hiếu.*

**2.** Hieu's parents teach him physics.
*Bố mẹ Hiếu dạy anh ấy môn vật lý.*

**3.** When Hieu needs help, his brother always helps him.
*Khi Hiếu cần giúp đỡ, anh trai luôn giúp.*

**4.** Hieu's family routines help them spend some time together every week.
*Các thói quen sinh hoạt giúp gia đình dành thời gian bên nhau mỗi tuần.*
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="0" href="audio/tts_29c83a10.mp3">
Ok đáp án nè.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Đáp án:** 1. F | 2. F | 3. T | 4. T
</answer>

<explanation>
**1. F (Sai)** - Hiếu nói "I live with my parents and my brother" = sống với bố mẹ và anh trai => có 4 người chứ không phải 3.

**2. F (Sai)** - Hiếu nói "My parents are farmers. They know little about physics." = Bố mẹ là nông dân, biết rất ít về vật lý => họ không dạy vật lý cho Hiếu. Họ chỉ tin tưởng, lắng nghe và động viên thôi.

**3. T (Đúng)** - Hiếu nói "My brother is my best friend... is always there for me when I need help." = Anh trai luôn ở bên khi cần giúp đỡ.

**4. T (Đúng)** - Hiếu nói "My parents have created some family routines so that every week we can spend some time together." = Bố mẹ tạo thói quen sinh hoạt để mỗi tuần dành thời gian bên nhau.
</explanation>

*________ và niềm tin của bố mẹ cho bạn ấy sức mạnh để tiếp tục.*

**3.** Hieu's brother is his best friend. He shares his happy and ________ moments with him.

</answer>

<explanation>

**3. sad** - "He shares his happy and sad moments with me." (Anh ấy chia sẻ khoảnh khắc vui buồn với tôi.)
