### Bài 1 trang 26 - Matching

<teacher_script pause="60">

<task>
**Đề:** Match the suggestions for improving the environment with their expected results.
**Dịch đề:** Nối các đề xuất cải thiện môi trường với kết quả mong đợi.
</task>

<questions type="matching">
**Suggestions:**
1. Cleaning up regularly (e.g. organising clean-up activities at weekends)
2. Planting more trees (e.g. encouraging students to plant trees in school)
3. Setting up more rubbish bins (e.g. putting more bins in public places)

**Expected results:**
a. This reduces CO2 and makes the air clean.
b. This makes the waste collection easier.
c. This keeps the environment clean and safe for everyone.
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Ok đáp án nè.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Đáp án:** 1-c | 2-a | 3-b
</answer>

<explanation>
**1-c:** Dọn dẹp thường xuyên → giữ môi trường sạch và an toàn cho mọi người.
**2-a:** Trồng thêm cây → giảm CO2 và làm không khí trong lành.
**3-b:** Đặt thêm thùng rác → thu gom rác dễ dàng hơn.
</explanation>

### Bài 2 trang 26 - Gap Fill

<teacher_script pause="45">
Bài 2: Điền <eng>First, For example, In conclusion</eng> vào đoạn văn nha. 45 giây.
</teacher_script>

<task>
**Đề:** Read the incomplete paragraph. Fill in the blanks with the words and phrases from the box.
**Dịch đề:** Đọc đoạn văn chưa hoàn chỉnh. Điền từ vào chỗ trống.
</task>

<questions type="fill_blanks">
| First | In conclusion | For example |

There are three things we can do to improve the environment in our school. (1) ________, we should clean up all areas regularly. (2) ________, we can organise clean-up activities at weekends. By doing this, we can keep our environment clean, beautiful, and safe for everyone. ......

(3) ________, we can improve the environment in our school in many ways and even small actions can make a big difference.
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Ok đáp án nè.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Đáp án:** 1. First | 2. For example | 3. In conclusion
</answer>

<explanation>
**(1) First** - Từ nối thứ tự: đầu tiên, mở đầu ý chính.
**(2) For example** - Đưa ra ví dụ cụ thể cho ý chính.
**(3) In conclusion** - Kết luận đoạn văn.

Cấu trúc đoạn văn: First (ý 1) + For example (ví dụ) → Second (ý 2) + For example → In conclusion (kết luận).
</explanation>

### Bài 3 trang 26 - Writing Practice

<teacher_script pause="300" action="photo">
Bài 3: Viết thêm 2 đề xuất cải thiện môi trường trường học nha. Dùng cấu trúc: <eng>Second... For example/For instance... In addition... For example...</eng> Viết xong chụp gửi. 5 phút.

<task>
**Đề:** Add two more suggestions to improve the environment in your school to the paragraph in 2.
**Dịch đề:** Thêm hai gợi ý khác để cải thiện môi trường trường học.
</task>

<answer>
**Bài mẫu:**

There are three things we can do to improve the environment in our school. First, we should clean up all areas regularly. For example, we can organise clean-up activities at weekends. By doing this, we can keep our environment clean, beautiful, and safe for everyone. Second, we ought to set up more rubbish bins. For instance, we can put more rubbish bins at the school gate, in front of each building, or in the playground. Thereby, we can make the waste collection easier. In addition, we should plant more trees. For example, we can encourage students to plant trees or flowers in our school because this reduces CO2 and makes the air clean. In conclusion, we can improve the environment in our school in many ways and even small actions can make a big difference.
</answer>
