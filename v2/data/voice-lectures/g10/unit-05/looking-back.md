### Grammar - Circle Correct Answers

<teacher_script pause="60">
Phần ngữ pháp: Khoanh tròn đáp án đúng nha. Nhớ: thì hiện tại hoàn thành, V-ing và to V. 1 phút.

<task>
**Đề:** Circle the correct answers.
**Dịch đề:** Khoanh tròn đáp án đúng.
</task>

<questions type="multiple_choice">
**1.** They ________ some interesting software on the school computers. The programmes are working very well and everyone enjoys ________ them.
- A. just installed - to use
- B. have just installed - using

**2.** Smartphones allow people ________ information over long distances. ________ with a smartphone is fun as well.
- A. sending - Learn
- B. to send - To learn

**3.** Since television ________, TV designs ________ a lot.
- A. was invented - have changed
- B. has been invented - changed
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Ok đáp án nè.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Đáp án:** 1. B | 2. B | 3. A
</answer>

<explanation>
**1. B - have just installed / using**
- "just" là dấu hiệu thì hiện tại hoàn thành: S + have/has + just + V3.
- "enjoy + V-ing" (thích làm gì): everyone enjoys USING them.

**2. B - to send / To learn**
- "allow + O + to V" (cho phép ai làm gì): allow people TO SEND.
- "To V" làm chủ ngữ đứng đầu câu: TO LEARN with a smartphone is fun.

**3. A - was invented / have changed**
- Cấu trúc: S + have/has + V3 + since + S + V-ed (bị động QKĐ).
- "Since television WAS INVENTED" (quá khứ đơn bị động) → "TV designs HAVE CHANGED" (hiện tại hoàn thành).
</explanation>
