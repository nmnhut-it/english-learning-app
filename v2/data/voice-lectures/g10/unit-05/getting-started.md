### Bài 4 trang 53 - Fill in the blanks

<questions type="fill_blanks">
Phong and his father are discussing what to buy for his studies. Phong has wanted a laptop or a smartphone (1) ________ a long time because they are both very useful. Laptops (2) ________ completely changed our lives since their invention but smartphones have also (3) ________ the way we work and study. For example, it's fun (4) ________ study with educational apps on smartphones. They allow students (5) ________ (6) ________ and communicate at the same time.
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Ok đáp án nè.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Đáp án:** 1. for | 2. have | 3. improved | 4. to | 5. to | 6. learn
</answer>

<explanation>
**1. for** - "for a long time" (trong một thời gian dài) - dấu hiệu thì hiện tại hoàn thành.
**2. have** - "have completely changed" - thì hiện tại hoàn thành.
**3. improved** - "have also improved" - thì hiện tại hoàn thành, cải thiện.
**4. to** - "it's fun to study" - cấu trúc It's + adj + to V.
**5. to, 6. learn** - "allow students to learn" - cấu trúc allow + O + to V.
</explanation>
