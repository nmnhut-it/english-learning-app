<questions type="multiple_choice">

### Bài 3 trang 92 - Writing

<teacher_script pause="300">
Bài 3, viết đoạn văn 120 đến 150 từ về lợi ích của học kết hợp. Dùng cấu trúc: mở bài giới thiệu, thân bài 3 lợi ích (First, Second, Finally), kết bài tóm tắt. 5 phút nha.
</teacher_script>

<task>
**Đề:** Write a paragraph (120-150 words) about the benefits of blended learning.
**Dịch đề:** Viết đoạn văn (120-150 từ) về lợi ích của học kết hợp.
</task>

<answer>
**Bài mẫu:**
Blended learning, which is a combination of face-to-face and online learning, has three main benefits. First, it helps students have more control over their own learning. Students can watch videos and read materials at home at their own pace, then discuss the topics in class. Second, it is easier for students to get access to lesson materials. They can read them online at any time. If students don't understand the lesson, they can rewatch the videos until they do. Finally, students can develop better communication and teamwork skills because they often have to discuss topics, exchange ideas, and work together on projects in class. In summary, blended learning is a very useful way of learning that combines the advantages of both face-to-face and online learning.
</answer>
