**UNIT 5: FOOD AND DRINK**

**A CLOSER LOOK 2** 

**some, a lot of và lots of** 

some: một vài, một ít  

a lot of \= lots of: nhiều 

**Cả hai đều đi với danh từ đếm được lẫn danh từ không đếm được**

**Bài 1 trang 53** 

1- a lot of

2- lots of

3-some

4- a lot of

5\. some

**Bài 2 trang 53** 

1- some/a lot of

2-any

3-some

4\. lots of

5-a lot of

**Bài 3 trang 54: Điền how much và how many**

how many \+ danh từ đếm được số nhiều: 

- 2.books  
- 3\. films   
- 4\. bananas   
- 5\. spring rolls

how much \+ danh từ không đếm được: 1\. water 

**Bài 3,4 trang 54** 

1\. How much water do we need every day? 

→ Two litres/We need/drink two litres. 

2\. How many books did you read last month?

→ We read two books. 

3\. How many films did you watch last year ?

→ I watched one film. 

4\. How many bananas do you eat every week? 

→ I eat five bananas every week. 

5\. How many spring rolls did your mother cook last month?

→ She cooked 100 spring rolls. 

**Bài 5 trang 54\.** 

1. pancake 

A: What do we need to make pancakes? 

B: We need eggs, sugar, flour, milk and butter. 

A: How many eggs do we need?

B: We need two

A: How much sugar do we need?

B. We need a tablespoon. 

A: How much flour do we need?

B: We need five tablespoons. 

A. How much milk do we need?

B: We need 100 ml. 

A: How much butter do we need? 

B: We need 15g. 

2. an omelette

A: What do we need to make an omelette ? 

B: We need eggs, water, butter, salt and pepper. 

A: How many eggs do we need?

B: We need three	

A: How much water do we need?

B. We need a tablespoon \= muỗng ăn cơm

A: How much butter (bơ) do we need?

B: We need 30g 

A. How much salt (muối /son.t/) do we need?

B: We need a teaspoon.

A: How much pepper (tiêu /pép pơ/) do we need? 

B: We need a teaspoon \= muỗng cà phê
