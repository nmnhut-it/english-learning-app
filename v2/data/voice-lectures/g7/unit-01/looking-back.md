### Bài 1 trang 18 - Identify hobbies from descriptions

<task>
**Đề:** Read the descriptions and write the correct hobby.
**Dịch đề:** Đọc mô tả và viết tên sở thích đúng.
</task>

<questions type="fill_blanks">
**1.** You keep small round metal pieces from different countries. What is your hobby?
*Bạn giữ những mảnh kim loại tròn nhỏ từ các nước khác nhau. Sở thích của bạn là gì?*
→ ___________________________

**2.** You wear a special suit and learn to throw and fall safely. What is your hobby?
*Bạn mặc đồ đặc biệt và học cách ném và ngã an toàn. Sở thích của bạn là gì?*
→ ___________________________

**3.** You carefully put together small plastic pieces to create a plane, a ship, or a car. What is your hobby?
*Bạn cẩn thận lắp ráp các mảnh nhựa nhỏ để tạo ra máy bay, tàu thủy, hoặc ô tô. Sở thích của bạn là gì?*
→ ___________________________

**4.** You grow vegetables and flowers outside your house. You water them every day. What is your hobby?
*Bạn trồng rau và hoa bên ngoài nhà. Bạn tưới nước mỗi ngày. Sở thích của bạn là gì?*
→ ___________________________

**5.** You and your friends kick a round ball and try to score goals. What is your hobby?
*Bạn và bạn bè đá một quả bóng tròn và cố gắng ghi bàn. Sở thích của bạn là gì?*
→ ___________________________
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Đáp án nha.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Bài 1 - Đáp án:**
1. collecting coins
2. doing judo
3. making models
4. gardening
5. playing football
</answer>

<explanation>
**1. collecting coins** — "small round metal pieces from different countries" = tiền xu từ nhiều nước → sưu tập tiền xu.

**2. doing judo** — "special suit, throw and fall safely" = mặc võ phục, ném và ngã → tập judo.

**3. making models** — "small plastic pieces to create a plane/ship/car" = lắp mô hình máy bay, tàu, xe → làm mô hình.

**4. gardening** — "grow vegetables and flowers, water every day" = trồng rau hoa, tưới nước → làm vườn.

**5. playing football** — "kick a round ball, score goals" = đá bóng, ghi bàn → chơi bóng đá.
</explanation>

### Bài 2 trang 18 - Write sentences with like/enjoy/love/hate

<task>
**Đề:** Write sentences about people's hobbies using the words in brackets.
**Dịch đề:** Viết câu về sở thích của mọi người dùng các từ trong ngoặc.
</task>

<questions type="complete_sentences">
**1.** Nam / like / collect / stamps
→ ___________________________

**2.** My sister / enjoy / do / yoga / every evening
→ ___________________________

**3.** Peter / doesn't like / ride / a horse
→ ___________________________

**4.** Our teacher / love / garden / at the weekend
→ ___________________________

**5.** They / hate / make / models / because it is difficult
→ ___________________________
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Xem đáp án nha.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Bài 2 - Đáp án:**
1. Nam likes collecting stamps.
2. My sister enjoys doing yoga every evening.
3. Peter doesn't like riding a horse.
4. Our teacher loves gardening at the weekend.
5. They hate making models because it is difficult.
</answer>

<explanation>
**1.** "Nam" → số ít → like + **s** = likes. Like + V-ing → likes **collecting**.

**2.** "My sister" → số ít → enjoy + **s** = enjoys. Enjoy + V-ing → enjoys **doing** yoga.

**3.** "Peter" → số ít → **doesn't** like + V-ing → doesn't like **riding**.

**4.** "Our teacher" → số ít → love + **s** = loves. Love + V-ing → loves **gardening**.

**5.** "They" → số nhiều → hate (không thêm s). Hate + V-ing → hate **making**.
</explanation>

### Bài 3 trang 18 - Fill in with Present Simple

<task>
**Đề:** Complete the passage with the correct form of the verbs in brackets.
**Dịch đề:** Hoàn thành đoạn văn với dạng đúng của động từ trong ngoặc.
</task>

<questions type="fill_blanks">
Phong __(1)__ (love) football. He __(2)__ (have) a lot of football cards. He __(3)__ (enjoy) collecting them. His parents __(4)__ (not like) football much but they always support him. His mum __(5)__ (be) a doctor and his dad __(6)__ (be) a teacher. Every Saturday, they __(7)__ (go) to watch Phong play. The match usually __(8)__ (begin) at 4 pm.
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Đáp án nha.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Bài 3 - Đáp án:** 1. loves | 2. has | 3. enjoys | 4. don't like | 5. is | 6. is | 7. go | 8. begins
</answer>

<explanation>
**1. loves** — "Phong" (he) → số ít → love + **s** = loves.

**2. has** — "He" → số ít → have → **has** (dạng đặc biệt).

**3. enjoys** — "He" → số ít → enjoy + **s** = enjoys.

**4. don't like** — "His parents" → số nhiều → **don't** like.

**5. is** — "His mum" → số ít → be → **is**.

**6. is** — "his dad" → số ít → be → **is**.

**7. go** — "they" → số nhiều → go (không thêm -s).

**8. begins** — "The match" → số ít → begin + **s** = begins.
</explanation>

### Bài 4 trang 19 - Questions and Negatives

<task>
**Đề:** Rewrite each sentence as a question and then as a negative sentence.
**Dịch đề:** Viết lại mỗi câu thành câu hỏi rồi câu phủ định.
</task>

<questions type="complete_sentences">
**1.** She enjoys collecting foreign coins.
→ Question: ___________________________?
→ Negative: ___________________________.

**2.** His sister does yoga every morning.
→ Question: ___________________________?
→ Negative: ___________________________.

**3.** They go jogging in the park on weekends.
→ Question: ___________________________?
→ Negative: ___________________________.

**4.** The football match starts at 4 pm on Saturday.
→ Question: ___________________________?
→ Negative: ___________________________.

**5.** My brother likes building dollhouses.
→ Question: ___________________________?
→ Negative: ___________________________.
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Ok đáp án nha. Xem từng câu kỹ kỹ.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Bài 4 - Đáp án:**

1. Does she enjoy collecting foreign coins? / She doesn't enjoy collecting foreign coins.
2. Does his sister do yoga every morning? / His sister doesn't do yoga every morning.
3. Do they go jogging in the park on weekends? / They don't go jogging in the park on weekends.
4. Does the football match start at 4 pm on Saturday? / The football match doesn't start at 4 pm on Saturday.
5. Does my brother like building dollhouses? / My brother doesn't like building dollhouses.
</answer>

<explanation>
**1.** "She" → số ít → **Does** she enjoy...? / She **doesn't** enjoy... (enjoy không thêm -s sau doesn't)

**2.** "His sister" → số ít → **Does** his sister **do** yoga...? (chú ý: "do yoga" — "do" là động từ chính, "does" là trợ động từ) / His sister **doesn't do** yoga...

**3.** "They" → số nhiều → **Do** they go...? / They **don't** go...

**4.** "The football match" → số ít → **Does** the football match **start**...? / The football match **doesn't start**... (start không có -s sau doesn't)

**5.** "My brother" → số ít → **Does** my brother like...? / My brother **doesn't like**...
</explanation>
