### On tap ngu phap 1: Thi tuong lai don (Future Simple - Will/Won't)

<teacher_script pause="0">
Truoc khi lam bai 3 va 4, minh on lai ngu phap nha. Dau tien la thi tuong lai don voi <eng>will</eng> va <eng>won't</eng>.
</teacher_script>

<grammar>
**THI TUONG LAI DON (Future Simple)**

| Dang | Cong thuc | Vi du |
|------|-----------|-------|
| (+) | S + will + V nguyen the | We **will travel** to new planets. |
| (-) | S + won't + V nguyen the | Cars **won't use** petrol. |
| (?) | Will + S + V nguyen the? | **Will** they **fly** to school? |

**Khi nao dung Future Simple?**
- Du doan ve tuong lai: *We **will travel** by flying car.*
- Quyet dinh ngay luc noi: *I **will help** you.*
- Loi hua, de nghi: *I **will call** you later.*
- Dau hieu nhan biet: tomorrow, next week, in the future, soon, I think...

**Luu y:** will + V nguyen the (KHONG chia, KHONG them s/es/ing)
</grammar>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Nho nha: will + dong tu nguyen the, khong chia gi het. Won't la dang phu dinh cua will. Gio minh on tiep dai tu so huu.
</teacher_script>

<!-- chunk: grammar-review-2 -->
### On tap ngu phap 2: Dai tu so huu (Possessive Pronouns)

<teacher_script pause="0">
Tiep theo la dai tu so huu va tinh tu so huu nha. Phan nay quan trong lam, cac em chu y.
</teacher_script>

<grammar>
**DAI TU SO HUU (Possessive Pronouns) vs TINH TU SO HUU (Possessive Adjectives)**

| Chu ngu (Subject) | Tinh tu so huu (Poss. Adj) | Dai tu so huu (Poss. Pronoun) |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| I | my | mine |
| you | your | yours |
| he | his | his |
| she | her | hers |
| we | our | ours |
| they | their | theirs |

**Cach phan biet:**
- **Tinh tu so huu** + danh tu: *This is **my** car.* (my + car)
- **Dai tu so huu** thay the cho "tinh tu so huu + danh tu": *This car is **mine**.* (mine = my car)

**Vi du:**
- Her bike is red. **Mine** is blue. (Mine = My bike)
- Our car runs on petrol. **Theirs** runs on electricity. (Theirs = Their car)
</grammar>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Nho nha: Tinh tu so huu luon di voi danh tu phia sau. Dai tu so huu thay the cho ca cum "tinh tu so huu + danh tu" nen dung mot minh. Gio minh lam bai 3.
</teacher_script>

### Bai 3 trang 121 - Possessive Pronouns

<teacher_script pause="0">
Bai 3 <eng>Find ONE mistake in each sentence and correct it</eng>. Tim MOT loi sai trong moi cau va sua lai nha. Bai nay on lai dai tu so huu va tinh tu so huu.
</teacher_script>

<task>
**De:** Find ONE mistake in each sentence and correct it.
**Dich de:** Tim MOT loi sai trong moi cau va sua no.
</task>

<questions type="fill_blanks">
**1.** My motorbike is an electric one. Her is not. __________
*Xe may cua toi la xe dien. Xe cua co ay thi khong.*

**2.** Her car can carry four passengers. Our can carry seven passengers. _______
*Xe cua co ay co the cho bon hanh khach. Xe cua chung toi co the cho bay hanh khach.*

**3.** Their car runs on petrol. My runs on solar energy. _________
*Xe cua ho chay bang xang. Xe cua toi chay bang nang luong mat troi.*

**4.** We go to school together. I ride my bike, and he rides its. ________
*Chung toi di hoc cung nhau. Toi di xe dap cua toi, va anh ay di xe cua anh ay.*

**5.** My city has a skyTran system. Does yours city have one? _________
*Thanh pho cua toi co he thong skyTran. Thanh pho cua ban co mot he thong nhu vay khong?*
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="60">
Doc tung cau, tim loi sai ve tinh tu so huu hoac dai tu so huu roi sua lai. Nho: tinh tu so huu + danh tu, dai tu so huu dung mot minh. 1 phut nha.
</teacher_script>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Ok dap an ne. Co giai thich tung cau luon.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Dap an:**
1. Her → Hers
2. Our → Ours
3. My → Mine
4. its → his
5. yours → your

**Giai thich chi tiet:**

**Cau 1:** Her → **Hers**
My motorbike is an electric one. **Hers** is not.
*(Xe may cua toi la xe dien. Xe cua co ay thi khong.)*
→ "Her" la tinh tu so huu, bat buoc phai co danh tu theo sau. O day khong co danh tu phia sau (thay the cho "Her motorbike"), nen phai dung dai tu so huu "Hers".

**Cau 2:** Our → **Ours**
Her car can carry four passengers. **Ours** can carry seven passengers.
*(Xe cua co ay co the cho bon hanh khach. Xe cua chung toi co the cho bay hanh khach.)*
→ "Our" la tinh tu so huu, can danh tu theo sau. O day thay the cho "Our car", nen phai dung dai tu so huu "Ours".

**Cau 3:** My → **Mine**
Their car runs on petrol. **Mine** runs on solar energy.
*(Xe cua ho chay bang xang. Xe cua toi chay bang nang luong mat troi.)*
→ "My" la tinh tu so huu, can danh tu theo sau. O day thay the cho "My car", nen phai dung dai tu so huu "Mine".

**Cau 4:** its → **his**
We go to school together. I ride my bike, and he rides **his**.
*(Chung toi di hoc cung nhau. Toi di xe dap cua toi, va anh ay di xe cua anh ay.)*
→ "its" dung cho vat. Chu ngu la "he" (nguoi), nen dai tu so huu tuong ung phai la "his" (= his bike).

**Cau 5:** yours → **your**
My city has a skyTran system. Does **your** city have one?
*(Thanh pho cua toi co he thong skyTran. Thanh pho cua ban co mot he thong nhu vay khong?)*
→ Phia sau co danh tu "city", nen phai dung tinh tu so huu "your", khong dung dai tu so huu "yours".
</answer>

<questions type="fill_blanks">

**2.** (go) __________
**3.** (need) __________
**4.** (carry) __________
**5.** will ... (come) __________
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="60">
Doc doan van, dien dang tuong lai don cua dong tu trong ngoac vao cho trong. Nho cong thuc: will + V nguyen the. 1 phut nha.
</teacher_script>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Ok dap an ne. Co giai thich luon.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Dap an:** 1. will travel | 2. will go | 3. will need | 4. will carry | 5. will - come

**Giai thich chi tiet:**

**Cho 1:** will travel
→ "In the future" va "Some scientists think..." dien ta du doan ve tuong lai → dung will + V nguyen the.

**Cho 2:** will go
→ Tiep tuc noi ve viec di chuyen trong tuong lai → will go.

**Cho 3:** will need
→ Du doan rang chung ta se can phuong tien dac biet moi → will need.

**Cho 4:** will carry
→ Mo ta dac diem cua phuong tien trong tuong lai → will carry.

**Cho 5:** will ... come
→ Day la cau hoi tuong lai don: "but **will** many our dreams **come** true soon?" - "will" da co san o dau cau hoi, chi can dien "come" vao cho trong.
</answer>

<translation>
**Dich doan van:**
Trong tuong lai, chung ta se di xa hon nhieu so voi hien tai. Chung ta cung se den tham nhung dia diem moi cho ky nghi cua chung ta. Mot so nha khoa hoc cho rang chung ta se du hanh den cac hanh tinh moi. Chung ta se den do bang tau vu tru, nhung chung ta se can nhung phuong tien van chuyen dac biet moi. Nhung phuong tien giao thong moi nay se cho mot hoac hai hanh khach, nhung nhieu uoc mo cua chung ta se som thanh hien thuc duoc khong? Chung ta hay cho xem.
</translation>
