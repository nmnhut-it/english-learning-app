### Grammar Review - Although / However

<teacher_script pause="0">
Truoc khi lam bai 3 va 4, on lai ngu phap nha.
</teacher_script>

<grammar>
**1. Although / Though / Even though (Mac du):**
- Dung de noi hai menh de trong cung mot cau
- Cau truc: Although + S + V, S + V
- Vi du: Although he arrived late, he left the cinema early.
*(Mac du anh ay den muon, anh ay roi rap som.)*

**2. However (Tuy nhien):**
- Dung de noi hai cau rieng biet
- Thuong dung dau cau thu hai, co dau cham truoc no
- Vi du: The film received good reviews. However, only a few people saw it.
*(Phim nhan duoc danh gia tot. Tuy nhien, chi co mot so nguoi xem.)*
</grammar>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Nho nha: <eng>Although</eng> dung noi hai menh de trong cung mot cau, dung dau cau. Con <eng>However</eng> thuong dung dau cau thu hai, co dau cham truoc no. Ca hai deu dien ta y nghia tuong phan - mac du, tuy nhien.
</teacher_script>

### Bai 4 trang 90 - Match sentences (Although/However)

<teacher_script pause="60">
Bai 4 <eng>Match the sentences</eng> nha. Noi cau o cot A voi cot B di. Chu y cach dung <eng>Although</eng> va <eng>However</eng> nha. 1 phut nha.
</teacher_script>

<task>
**De:** Match the sentences or sentence halves in columns A and B.
**Dich de:** Noi cac cau hoac mot ve cau trong cot A voi B.
</task>

<questions type="matching">
**A:**
1. Although he arrived late,
*Mac du anh ay den muon,*

2. The film received good reviews.
*Phim nhan duoc nhieu danh gia tot.*

3. Though popcorn and other snacks in the cinema are very expensive,
*Mac du bong ngo va cac mon an nhe khac trong rap chieu phim rat dat,*

4. Cinema tickets are expensive.
*Ve xem phim rat dat.*

5. Although I don't really like to go to the cinema,
*Mac du toi khong that su thich di xem phim,*

**B:**
a. people still buy them.
*moi nguoi van mua chung.*

b. However, the number of people going to cinemas is increasing.
*Tuy nhien, luong nguoi den rap chieu phim van ngay cang dong.*

c. I don't want to stay home tonight.
*toi khong muon o nha toi nay.*

d. he left the cinema early.
*anh ay da roi rap chieu phim som.*

e. However, only a few people saw it.
*Tuy nhien, chi co mot so nguoi xem no.*
</questions>

---

<!-- chunk: answer_4 -->
<teacher_script pause="0">
Da het thoi gian. Ok dap an ne.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Dap an:** 1.d | 2.e | 3.a | 4.b | 5.c
</answer>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Cau 1 noi voi d. "Although he arrived late, he left the cinema early" - Mac du anh ay den tre, anh ay roi rap som. Tuong phan: <eng>arrived late</eng> - den tre nhung <eng>left early</eng> - ve som. Co le phim khong hay nen ve som a.
</teacher_script>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Cau 2 noi voi e. "The film received good reviews. However, only a few people saw it" - Phim nhan duoc danh gia tot. Tuy nhien, chi co mot so nguoi xem no. Tuong phan: <eng>good reviews</eng> - danh gia tot nhung <eng>only a few people saw it</eng> - chi it nguoi xem. Hoi la nhi.
</teacher_script>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Cau 3 noi voi a. "Though popcorn and other snacks in the cinema are very expensive, people still buy them" - Mac du bong ngo va do an vat trong rap rat dat, nhieu nguoi van mua. Tuong phan: <eng>expensive</eng> - dat nhung <eng>still buy</eng> - van mua. Dat ma van mua vi thich a.
</teacher_script>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Cau 4 noi voi b. "Cinema tickets are expensive. However, the number of people going to cinemas is increasing" - Ve rap dat. Tuy nhien, luong nguoi den rap ngay cang tang. Day dung <eng>However</eng> de noi hai cau rieng biet, co dau cham truoc <eng>However</eng> nha. Tuong phan: ve dat nhung nguoi di xem van tang.
</teacher_script>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Cau 5 noi voi c. "Although I don't really like to go to the cinema, I don't want to stay home tonight" - Mac du toi khong that su thich di rap, toi khong muon o nha toi nay. Tuong phan: <eng>don't like cinema</eng> - khong thich rap nhung <eng>don't want to stay home</eng> - khong muon o nha. Co le muon di choi cho khac a.
</teacher_script>

<explanation>
**Giai thich:**
1. **Although he arrived late** + d (he left early) -> Den tre + ve som (tuong phan).
2. **Good reviews** + e (However, only a few people saw it) -> Danh gia tot + it nguoi xem.
3. **Though... expensive** + a (people still buy them) -> Dat + van mua.
4. **Tickets expensive** + b (However, number increasing) -> Ve dat + nguoi tang.
5. **Although... don't like cinema** + c (don't want to stay home) -> Khong thich rap + khong muon o nha.
</explanation>
