**UNIT 10: ENERGY SOURCES**

**A CLOSER LOOK 2** 

**Trả bài**

**Thì hiện tại tiếp diễn**

**Câu khẳng định:** Chủ từ \+ am/is/are \+ V-ing \+ O 

**Câu phủ định:** Chủ từ \+ am/is/are not \+ V-ing \+ O 

is not \= isn’t; are not \= aren’t 

**Câu nghi vấn:** Am/is/are \+ chủ từ \+ V-ing \+ O?   
→ Yes, chủ từ \+ am/is/are.   
No, chủ từ \+ am not/isn’t/aren’t.

**Bài 4 trang 108**
