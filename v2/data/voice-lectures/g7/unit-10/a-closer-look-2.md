<grammar>
### THI HIEN TAI TIEP DIEN (The Present Continuous)

**1. Cau khang dinh (Affirmative):**
- I + **am** + V-ing
- He/She/It + **is** + V-ing
- You/We/They + **are** + V-ing

*Vi du:*
- I **am studying** English now. *(Toi dang hoc tieng Anh bay gio.)*
- She **is teaching** science. *(Co ay dang day khoa hoc.)*
- They **are playing** football. *(Ho dang choi bong da.)*

**2. Cau phu dinh (Negative):**
- Subject + am/is/are + **not** + V-ing
- is not = **isn't**
- are not = **aren't**

*Vi du:*
- I **am not watching** TV. *(Toi khong dang xem TV.)*
- She **isn't swimming** now. *(Co ay khong dang boi bay gio.)*
- They **aren't using** coal. *(Ho khong dang su dung than.)*

**3. Cau nghi van (Question):**
- **Am/Is/Are** + Subject + V-ing?
- Yes, Subject + am/is/are.
- No, Subject + am not/isn't/aren't.

*Vi du:*
- **Are** you **studying**? - Yes, I am. / No, I'm not.
- **Is** she **cooking**? - Yes, she is. / No, she isn't.

**4. Dau hieu nhan biet:**
- now (bay gio)
- right now (ngay bay gio)
- at the moment (luc nay)
- at present (hien tai)
- nowadays (ngay nay)
- these days (nhung ngay nay)
- this week/month/year (tuan/thang/nam nay)
</grammar>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Nho cau truc nay nha: Subject cong am, is, are, cong V them ing. Meo nho nha: Am cho I, Is cho he she it, Are cho you we they. Nho cau "I am, He is, They are" la duoc. Gio lam bai tap di.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Dap an:** 1. is talking | 2. are using | 3. are taking | 4. are developing | 5. are reducing
</answer>

<explanation>
**Cau 1:** Mai **is talking** about types of energy sources now.
- Co "now" (bay gio) => dung thi hien tai tiep dien
- Chu ngu "Mai" la so it => is + V-ing
- talk => talking

**Cau 2:** We **are using** solar energy to replace energy from coal today.
- Co "today" (hom nay) => dung thi hien tai tiep dien
- Chu ngu "We" => are + V-ing
- use => using (bo e roi them ing)

**Cau 3:** Keep quiet! The students of Class 7C **are taking** a test.
- Hanh dong dang xay ra ngay luc noi => dung thi hien tai tiep dien
- Chu ngu "The students" la so nhieu => are + V-ing
- take => taking (bo e roi them ing)

**Cau 4:** Scientists **are developing** new energy sources to protect the environment.
- Hanh dong dang xay ra => dung thi hien tai tiep dien
- Chu ngu "Scientists" la so nhieu => are + V-ing
- develop => developing

**Cau 5:** We **are reducing** the use of nuclear energy nowadays.
- Co "nowadays" (ngay nay) => dung thi hien tai tiep dien
- Chu ngu "We" => are + V-ing
- reduce => reducing (bo e roi them ing)
</explanation>
