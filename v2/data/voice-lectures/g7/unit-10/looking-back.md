### On tap ngu phap: Present Simple vs Present Continuous

<teacher_script pause="0">
Truoc khi lam bai 3 va 4, minh on lai ngu phap ve thi hien tai don va hien tai tiep dien nha.
</teacher_script>

<grammar>
**THI HIEN TAI DON (Present Simple)**

| Dang | Cong thuc | Vi du |
|------|-----------|-------|
| (+) | S + V(s/es) | They **go** to school. / He **goes** to school. |
| (-) | S + don't/doesn't + V | They **don't go** to school. / He **doesn't go** to school. |
| (?) | Do/Does + S + V? | **Do** they **go** to school? / **Does** he **go** to school? |

**Khi nao dung Present Simple?**
- Dien ta thoi quen, so thich: *I **play** football every day.*
- Hanh dong lap di lap lai co chu ky: *She **visits** her grandma every Sunday.*
- Dau hieu nhan biet: always, usually, often, sometimes, never, every day/week/month...

---

**THI HIEN TAI TIEP DIEN (Present Continuous)**

| Dang | Cong thuc | Vi du |
|------|-----------|-------|
| (+) | S + am/is/are + V-ing | They **are talking**. / He **is talking**. / I **am talking**. |
| (-) | S + am/is/are + not + V-ing | They **are not talking**. / He **is not talking**. |
| (?) | Am/Is/Are + S + V-ing? | **Are** they **talking**? / **Is** he **talking**? |

**Khi nao dung Present Continuous?**
- Hanh dong dang xay ra ngay luc noi: *I **am writing** an essay now.*
- Sau cac tu thu hut su chu y: Look! Listen! Watch out!
- Hanh dong bat thuong, tam thoi: *You **are being** so nice today!*
- Dau hieu nhan biet: now, at the moment, at present, Look!, Listen!...
</grammar>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Nho nha: Hien tai don cho thoi quen, lap di lap lai. Hien tai tiep dien cho hanh dong dang xay ra ngay bay gio. Gio minh lam bai 3.
</teacher_script>

### Bai 3 trang 112 - Present Simple or Present Continuous

<teacher_script pause="0">
Bai 3 <eng>Complete the sentences by using the correct form of the present continuous or present simple of the verbs in brackets</eng>. Hoan thanh cau bang cach dung dung dang cua thi hien tai tiep dien hoac hien tai don nha.
</teacher_script>

<task>
**De:** Complete the sentences by using the correct form of the present continuous or present simple of the verbs in brackets.
**Dich de:** Hoan thanh cac cau bang cach su dung dung dang cua thi hien tai tiep dien hoac hien tai don cua cac dong tu trong ngoac.
</task>

<questions type="fill_blanks">
**1.** Look! It (rain) ________ heavily.
*Nhin kia! Troi (mua) ________ lon.*

**2.** Normally they (start) ________ school at eight o'clock in the morning.
*Thuong thi ho (bat dau) ________ di hoc luc tam gio sang.*

**3.** He hasn't got a bike at the moment, so he (walk) ________ to school this week.
*Anh ay khong co xe dap vao luc nay, nen anh ay (di bo) ________ den truong tuan nay.*

**4.** He always (do) ________ his homework in the evening.
*Anh ay luon (lam) ________ bai tap ve nha vao buoi toi.*

**5.** I'm afraid I have no time to help just now. I (write) ________ an essay.
*Toi so rang toi khong co thoi gian de giup luc nay. Toi (viet) ________ mot bai luan.*
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="60">
Doc tung cau, tim dau hieu nhan biet thi, roi chia dong tu trong ngoac cho dung. 1 phut nha.
</teacher_script>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Ok dap an ne. Co giai thich tung cau.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Bài 3 - Đáp án:** 1. is raining | 2. start | 3. is walking | 4. does | 5. am writing

**Giải thích chi tiết:**

**Cau 1:** Look! It **is raining** heavily.
*(Nhin kia! Troi dang mua to.)*
→ "Look!" la tu thu hut su chu y → dung **thi hien tai tiep dien**: S + is/am/are + V-ing

**Cau 2:** Normally they **start** school at eight o'clock in the morning.
*(Thuong thi ho bat dau di hoc luc tam gio sang.)*
→ "Normally" (thuong thuong) → dung **thi hien tai don** de dien ta thoi quen: S + V(s/es)

**Cau 3:** He hasn't got a bike at the moment, so he **is walking** to school this week.
*(Anh ay khong co xe dap vao luc nay, nen anh ay dang di bo den truong tuan nay.)*
→ "at the moment" (ngay luc nay) → dung **thi hien tai tiep dien**: S + is/am/are + V-ing

**Cau 4:** He always **does** his homework in the evening.
*(Anh ay luon lam bai tap ve nha vao buoi toi.)*
→ "always" (luon luon) → dung **thi hien tai don** de dien ta thoi quen: S + V(s/es). Chu y: He + does (them -es)

**Cau 5:** I'm afraid I have no time to help just now. I **am writing** an essay.
*(Toi e rang toi khong co thoi gian de giup ngay bay gio. Toi dang viet mot bai luan.)*
→ "just now" (ngay bay gio) → dung **thi hien tai tiep dien**: S + is/am/are + V-ing
</answer>

**3.** Nowadays, people in Africa is not using energy from natural gas.

**4.** They are use wind energy instead of coal.
*Ho dang su dung nang luong gio thay vi than da.*

**5.** We look for a cheap, clean and effective source of energy now.
*Chung toi tim kiem mot nguon nang luong re, sach va hieu qua bay gio.*
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="60">
Doc tung cau, tim loi sai ve ngu phap roi sua lai. Chu y cac dau hieu cua thi hien tai tiep dien nha. 1 phut.

<teacher_script pause="0">
Ok dap an ne. Co giai thich tung cau.

<answer>
**Bài 4 - Đáp án:**
1. do → are doing
2. is explain → is explaining
3. is → are
4. use → using
5. look → are looking

**Giai thich chi tiet:**

**Cau 1:** do → **are doing**
The students **are doing** the project in Unit 10 now.
*(Cac hoc sinh dang thuc hien du an trong Bai 10 ngay bay gio.)*
→ "now" (bay gio) → can dung **thi hien tai tiep dien**: are doing

**Cau 2:** is explain → **is explaining**
At the moment, the teacher **is explaining** how solar energy works.
*(Hien tai, giao vien dang giai thich cach hoat dong cua nang luong mat troi.)*
→ Cau truc hien tai tiep dien: is + V-ing. "explain" phai them "-ing" thanh "explaining"

**Cau 3:** is → **are**
Nowadays, people in Africa **are** not using energy from natural gas.
*(Ngay nay, nguoi dan o Chau Phi khong su dung nang luong tu khi dot tu nhien.)*
→ Chu ngu "people" la so nhieu → dung "are", khong dung "is"

**Cau 4:** use → **using**
They are **using** wind energy instead of coal.
*(Ho dang su dung nang luong gio thay vi than da.)*
→ Cau truc hien tai tiep dien: are + V-ing. "use" phai them "-ing" thanh "using"

**Cau 5:** look → **are looking**
We **are looking** for a cheap, clean and effective source of energy now.
*(Chung toi dang tim kiem mot nguon nang luong re, sach va hieu qua ngay bay gio.)*
→ "now" (bay gio) → can dung **thi hien tai tiep dien**: are looking
</answer>
