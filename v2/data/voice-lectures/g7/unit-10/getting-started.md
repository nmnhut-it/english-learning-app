**Bài 3 trang 105**
1. Lan thì đang làm cái gì? →\> She’s doing a project on energy sources.
2. Energy nghĩa là gì? → It’s the power that we use to provide us with light, heat or electricity.
3. Năng lượng đến từ đâu? → It comes from many sources like oil, coal, gas or even the sun and wind or water,
4. Những nguồn tái tạo được là gì? → They are sources that we cannot run out of. We can easily replace them.
