**UNIT 11: ELECTRONIC DEVICES****

**SKILLS 1**   
**Bài 4 trang 120**  
1\. What electronic device is important to you? (Thiết bị điện tử nào quan trọng với bạn?)  
My smartphone is my most important electronic device.  
2\. How long have you had it? (Bạn đã có nó bao lâu rồi?)  
I've had it for about two years.  
3\. What does it look like? (Nó trông như thế nào?)  
It has a large touchscreen with a black case. It's slim with 2 cameras on the back and 1 on the front.   
4\. When do you use it? (Khi nào bạn sử dụng nó?)  
I use it throughout the day for messaging, calls, browsing, taking photos, and entertainment.  
5\. Why is it important to you? (Tại sao nó quan trọng với bạn?)  
It keeps me connected with people, helps me organize tasks, provides information, and serves as my all-in-one device for communication and entertainment.
