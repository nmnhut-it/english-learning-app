**UNIT 11: ELECTRONIC DEVICES****

**A CLOSER LOOK 2**   
**“Tôi đề nghị bạn nên làm gì”**   
Chủ từ \+ suggest \+ V-ing  
Chủ từ \+ suggest \+ (that) \+ chủ từ 2 \+ V0  
**Ví dụ:** I suggest he be quiet.  
Có should để câu mượt hơn:  
	I suggest that you should be quiet. 

**Ngoài suggest, có thể dùng advice (khuyên), recommend (đề xuất)**
