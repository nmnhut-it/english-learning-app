Bánh chưng is one of the traditional dishes of Viet Nam. It originated in the 6th Hung King’s time. The basic ingredients include glutinous rice, green beans and pork. We usually eat banh chung at Tet or on the Hung King’s anniversary. We all like the flavor of it and it has become a symbol for Tet and the anniversary.
