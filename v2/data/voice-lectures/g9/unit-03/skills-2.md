## Tapescript: Student Time Management — Radio Talk

**Host:** Today we have three students sharing their time management strategies. Let's start with Lan.

**Lan:** Hi! I use a calendar app on my phone to plan ahead. I colour-code my tasks: red for urgent assignments due soon, yellow for important tasks, and green for flexible activities. Every Sunday evening, I sit down and arrange my whole week. This way, I never feel stressed about forgetting something.

**Host:** Great system, Lan! Now, Phong, what about you?

**Phong:** I like to study in the library after school. Being in a quiet place helps me minimise distractions — at home there's always something pulling my attention away. I also put my phone in my bag so I'm not tempted to check it every five minutes!

**Host:** Smart strategy! Finally, Tom, what's your approach?

**Tom:** I used to feel overwhelmed by big assignments. Then my teacher suggested breaking tasks into small steps. Instead of thinking "I need to write a 500-word essay," I think "Today I'll just write the introduction." It makes everything feel more manageable, and I accomplish more each day.

**Host:** Excellent advice from all three of you! Remember, finding a system that works for YOU is the most effective approach. Thanks for sharing!
</reading>

<questions type="multiple_choice">
**1.** What tool does Lan use to manage her time?
- A. A paper diary
- B. A sticky note board
- C. A calendar app on her phone

**2.** What does the colour RED mean in Lan's system?
- A. Urgent tasks due soon
- B. Important but flexible activities
- C. Finished tasks

**3.** Why does Phong go to the library to study?
- A. To focus better without distractions
- B. Because he doesn't have internet at home
- C. Because the library is closer to school

**4.** What does Tom's teacher suggest to avoid feeling overwhelmed?
- A. Break big tasks into small steps
- B. Study for longer hours
- C. Work on multiple tasks at the same time

**5.** According to the host, what is the most effective approach?
- A. Using a library
- B. Finding a system that works for you personally
- C. Colour-coding all your assignments
</questions>

<answer>
**Đáp án:** 1.C | 2.A | 3.A | 4.A | 5.B
</answer>

<explanation>
**1. C — A calendar app on her phone** — Lan nói: *"I use a calendar app on my phone to plan ahead."* Dùng ứng dụng lịch trên điện thoại.

**2. A — Urgent tasks due soon** — Lan nói: *"red for urgent assignments due soon."* Đỏ = khẩn cấp, sắp đến hạn nộp.

**3. A — To focus better without distractions** — Phong nói: *"helps me minimise distractions."* Học ở thư viện để giảm thiểu xao lãng, tập trung tốt hơn.

**4. A — Break big tasks into small steps** — Tom nói: *"breaking tasks into small steps."* Chia nhỏ nhiệm vụ lớn thành các bước nhỏ.

**5. B — Finding a system that works for you personally** — Host kết luận: *"finding a system that works for YOU is the most effective approach."* Mỗi người phù hợp với một cách khác nhau.

<task>
**Đề:** Write a time management plan (about 100 words). Include:
1. What tool/method you use (calendar, diary, app...)
2. How you prioritise tasks
3. How you deal with distractions
4. What your daily study routine looks like

**Dịch đề:** Viết kế hoạch quản lý thời gian (khoảng 100 từ). Bao gồm: công cụ em dùng, cách ưu tiên nhiệm vụ, cách xử lý xao lãng, và thói quen học tập hàng ngày của em.
</task>

<questions type="writing">
**Cấu trúc gợi ý:**

- **Câu 1-2:** Giới thiệu công cụ / phương pháp em dùng
- **Câu 3-4:** Cách em ưu tiên và sắp xếp bài vở
- **Câu 5-6:** Cách em tránh xao lãng
- **Câu 7-8:** Thói quen buổi tối và đi ngủ
</questions>

<answer>
**Bài mẫu:**

*"I manage my time by writing a weekly plan every Sunday evening. I use a notebook to list all my assignments and their due dates. I mark the most urgent tasks in red and try to finish them first. During study time, I put my phone in my bag to minimise distractions. I use the Pomodoro method — I study for 30 minutes, then take a 5-minute break. In the evening, I spend time with my family and avoid screens after 9pm so I can sleep well. This routine helps me feel less stressed and more accomplished each day."*

*(~100 từ — đủ cấu trúc)*
</answer>
