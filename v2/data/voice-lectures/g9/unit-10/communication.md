<grammar>
**Useful expressions for persuading:**
*(Cac mau cau de thuyet phuc)*

1. **Why don't you give it a go?** - Tai sao ban khong thu?
2. **How would you feel about + V-ing?** - Ban cam thay the nao ve viec...?
3. **Your contribution would really help us out.** - Su dong gop cua ban thuc su se giup ich cho chung toi.

**Useful responses:**
*(Cac cach tra loi)*

1. **OK, I'll think about that.** - Duoc roi, toi se suy nghi ve dieu do.
2. **Alright. I'll make a contribution.** - Duoc roi. Toi se dong gop.
</grammar>

### Bai 3 trang 108-109 - Reading and Matching

<task>
**De:** Read the short descriptions of various habitats and match them with their features.

**Dich de:** Doc nhung mo ta ngan gon ve cac moi truong song khac nhau va noi chung voi cac dac diem cua chung.
</task>

<reading>
**Polar habitats** include the North Pole and the South Pole. These habitats are covered in ice and extremely cold and dry. Animals here include small fish, polar bears, penguins, leopard seals, etc.

**Forest habitats** include tropical forests, temperate forests, and boreal forests. They are considered the Earth's lungs because they produce oxygen. They also provide homes to bats, owls, deer, squirrels, foxes, lizards, etc.

**Ocean habitats** include the Pacific, Atlantic, Indian, Southern, and Arctic Oceans. They produce more than 50 per cent of Earth's oxygen and help adjust the climate. They provide living places for plants like sea grasses, microscopic algae, and fish, etc.

**Grasslands** include tropical and temperate ones. The main plants are grasses. They are crucial for grazing livestock. Grassland animals include giraffes, zebras, lions, elephants, etc.
</reading>

<translation>
**Moi truong song vung cuc** bao gom Bac Cuc va Nam Cuc. Nhung moi truong song nay duoc bao phu trong bang va cuc ky lanh va kho. Dong vat o day bao gom ca nho, gau bac cuc, chim canh cut, hai cau bao, v.v.

**Moi truong song trong rung** bao gom rung nhiet doi, rung on doi va rung phuong bac. Chung duoc coi la la phoi cua Trai dat vi chung tao ra oxy. Chung cung cung cap nha cho doi, cu, huou, soc, cao, than lan, v.v.

**Moi truong song o dai duong** bao gom Thai Binh Duong, Dai Tay Duong, An Do Duong, Nam va Bac Bang Duong. Chung tao ra hon 50% luong oxy cua Trai dat va giup dieu chinh khi hau. Chung cung cap noi song cho cac loai thuc vat nhu co bien, tao cuc nho va ca, v.v.

**Dong co** bao gom dong co nhiet doi va on doi. Cay trong chu yeu la co. Chung rat quan trong de chan tha gia suc. Dong vat dong co bao gom huou cao co, ngua van, su tu, voi, v.v.

<answer>
**Sample report:**

While oceans are water bodies, forests are landform habitats. They share a critical role in sustaining life on Earth - they both provide oxygen to Earth, absorb carbon dioxide and regulate climate. Each has its own unique characteristics and importance. Forest habitats include tropical forests, temperate forests, and boreal forest; whereas ocean habitats include the Pacific, Atlantic, Indian, Southern, and Arctic Oceans.

Oceans are home to a diversity of marine life, from the smallest plankton to the largest whales. Coral reefs, found in tropical waters, are vibrant "underwater cities". Meanwhile, forests are teeming with biodiversity, with a treasure trove of flora and fauna, including trees, mammals, birds, insects, amphibians (frogs, salamanders), etc.

In conclusion, while oceans and forests may seem worlds apart, they are both ensuring the well-being of our planet and all its inhabitants. Let us cherish and protect these invaluable ecosystems for generations to come.

---

**Tam dich:**

*Trong khi dai duong la cac vung nuoc thi rung la moi truong song co dia hinh. Chung co vai tro quan trong trong viec duy tri su song tren Trai Dat - ca hai deu cung cap oxy cho Trai Dat, hap thu khi cac bon va dieu hoa khi hau. Moi cai deu co nhung dac diem va tam quan trong rieng. Moi truong song trong rung bao gom rung nhiet doi, rung on doi va rung phuong bac; trong khi moi truong song o dai duong bao gom Thai Binh Duong, Dai Tay Duong, An Do Duong, Nam va Bac Bang Duong.*

*Dai duong la noi sinh song cua nhieu loai sinh vat bien da dang, tu nhung sinh vat phu du nho nhat den nhung loai ca voi lon nhat. Cac ran san ho duoc tim thay o vung bien nhiet doi la nhung "thanh pho duoi nuoc" soi dong. Trong khi do, cac khu rung lai co rat nhieu su da dang sinh hoc, voi mot kho tang dong thuc vat, bao gom cay coi, dong vat co vu, chim, con trung, dong vat luong cu (ech, ky nhong), v.v.*

*Tom lai, mac du dai duong va rung duong nhu cach xa nhau nhung chung deu dam bao su song cho hanh tinh cua chung ta va tat ca cu dan tren do. Chung ta hay tran trong va bao ve nhung he sinh thai vo gia nay cho cac the he mai sau.*
</answer>
