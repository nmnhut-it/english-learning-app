### Bài 2 trang 80 - Grammar: Reported Speech

<teacher_script pause="90">
Bài 2. Viết lại các câu sau sang câu gián tiếp. 1 phút rưỡi.
</teacher_script>

<questions type="fill_blanks">
1. "Is it far from here?" she asked.
-> She asked ________________________________.

2. "Can I take a photo here?" Tom asked the guard.
-> Tom asked the guard ________________________________.

3. "Do you know the way to the beach?" he asked me.
-> He asked me ________________________________.
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Đáp án nhé.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Đáp án:**
1. She asked **if/whether it was** far from there.
2. Tom asked the guard **if/whether he could** take a photo there.
3. He asked me **if/whether I knew** the way to the beach.
</answer>

<explanation>
**Giải thích:**
- Nhớ lùi thì (is -> was, can -> could, do know -> knew).
- Đổi từ (here -> there).
- Thêm if/whether.
</explanation>
