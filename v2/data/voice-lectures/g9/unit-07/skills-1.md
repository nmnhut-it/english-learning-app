### Bài 2 trang 78 - Match words with meanings

<teacher_script pause="60">
Chúng ta cùng làm Bài 2 <eng>Match words with meanings</eng>. Các em hãy nối từ trong bài đọc với nghĩa thích hợp. 1 phút nha.

<questions type="matching">
**Words:**
1. majestic
2. heritage
3. cliffs
4. peaks

**Meanings:**
a. steep rocks
b. the pointed tops of mountains
c. features belonging to the culture of a particular society, such as traditions, languages, or buildings
d. beautiful, powerful, or causing great admiration
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="0">

</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Đáp án:** 1.d | 2.c | 3.a | 4.b
</answer>

### Bài 3 trang 78 - Read and answer

<teacher_script pause="120">
Chúng ta cùng làm Bài 3. Các em hãy đọc lại bài văn và trả lời các câu hỏi sau. 2 phút nha.
</teacher_script>

<questions type="open_ended">
**1.** Where are the Dolomites?
**2.** When were they recognised as a UNESCO World Heritage Site?
**3.** What can visitors see there?
**4.** What activities can tourists do in the Dolomites?
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Sau đây là gợi ý trả lời.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Gợi ý:**
1. They are in northeastern Italy.
2. In 2009.
3. They can see steep rocky cliffs, sharp peaks, and narrow, deep valleys.
4. They can go skiing, climbing, hiking, and cycling.
</answer>
