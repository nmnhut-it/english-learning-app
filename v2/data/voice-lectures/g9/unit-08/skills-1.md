### Bài 2 trang 26 - Reading Match

<teacher_script pause="120">
Bài 2 đọc đoạn văn và nối từ với định nghĩa của nó. 2 phút nha.
</teacher_script>

<reading>
**Package Tour vs Self-guided Tour**
...Package tour: agency takes care of almost everything... follow a **fixed** itinerary.
...Self-guided tour: require them to do everything on their own... **hunt for** tickets... **smooth** trip...
</reading>

<task>
**Match words:**
1. cost
2. fixed
3. hunt for
4. smooth

**Definitions:**
a. happening without problems
b. money spent
c. not changing
d. look for
</task>

<answer>
**Đáp án:**
1 - b (cost - money spent)
2 - c (fixed - not changing)
3 - d (hunt for - look for)
4 - a (smooth - happening without problems)
</answer>

### Bài 3 trang 26 - Answer questions

<teacher_script pause="120">
Bài 3 đọc lại và trả lời câu hỏi. 2 phút nha.
</teacher_script>

<questions type="open_ended">
**1.** What can a package tour save you?
**2.** What disadvantage of package tour?
**3.** What do travellers do on self-guided tour?
**4.** What is advantage of self-guided tours?
**5.** What skill should you have for self-guided tour?
</questions>

<answer>
**Đáp án gợi ý:**
1. Time and stress.
2. Follow a fixed itinerary.
3. Look for destination, work out itinerary, estimate cost, hunt for tickets.
4. Cheaper and more flexible. Freedom to decide.
5. Good at using apps.
</answer>
