<!-- chunk: grammar_theory -->
<grammar>
**Relative Pronouns:**
1. **who**: refers to people (chỉ người)
   - Example: I know a girl **who** works as a tour guide.
2. **which**: refers to things (chỉ vật)
   - Example: I'm reading a brochure **which** I picked up.
3. **whose**: refers to possession (chỉ sở hữu)
   - Example: The man **whose** book you are reading is my friend.
</grammar>

<teacher_script pause="0" href="audio/tts_509a0695.mp3">
Các em nhớ: **who** cho người, **which** cho vật, và **whose** biểu thị sở hữu.
</teacher_script>

2. The brochure
3. the video
4. a local tour guide

### Bài 2 trang 23 - Choose correct relative pronoun

<teacher_script pause="60" href="audio/tts_d5b1d729.mp3">
Các em hãy đọc kỹ yêu cầu và hoàn thành bài tập vào vở hoặc sách giáo khoa.
</teacher_script>

<questions type="multiple_choice">
**1.** This is the brochure **which / who** provides information...
**2.** ABC is the company **which / who** won the award...
**3.** The elders **which / who** are not good at using apps...
**4.** A hop-on hop-off bus **which / who** runs from here...
**5.** Food tourism is for tourists **which / who** want to explore...
</questions>

<answer>
**Đáp án:**
1. which (brochure - thing)
2. which (company - thing)
3. who (elders - people)
4. which (bus - thing)
5. who (tourists - people)
</answer>
