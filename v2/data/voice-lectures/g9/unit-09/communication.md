### Everyday English: Good luck!

<teacher_script pause="0">
Các em hãy nghe các mẫu câu sau.
</teacher_script>

<grammar>
**1. Chúc may mắn (Wishing good luck):**
- Good luck with your exam! (Chúc thi tốt!)
- Good luck!
- I wish you luck!
- Break a leg! (Thành ngữ: Chúc may mắn)

**2. Đáp lại (Responding):**
- Thanks. I'll need it.
- Thank you. I'll try my best.
- Thanks!
</grammar>
