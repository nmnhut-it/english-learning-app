<questions type="fill_blanks">
**Word bank:** *accent | bilingual | dialect | official | fluent*

1. She speaks English with a Scottish _______.
2. Singapore has four _______ languages.

### Bài 2 trang 40 - Grammar: Relative Clauses

<teacher_script pause="90">
Bài 2. Nối câu dùng mệnh đề quan hệ.
</teacher_script>

<questions type="fill_blanks">
1. The girl is my sister. You met her yesterday. (**who**)
-> The girl ________________________________.

2. This is the book. I bought it in London. (**which**)
-> This is the book ________________________________.

3. The boy is smart. His father is a teacher. (**whose**)
-> The boy ________________________________.
</questions>

<answer>
**Đáp án:**
1. The girl **who you met yesterday** is my sister.
2. This is the book **which I bought in London**.
3. The boy **whose father is a teacher** is smart.
</answer>
