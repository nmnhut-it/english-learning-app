### Bài 1 trang 26 - Complete conversation

<teacher_script pause="60">
Bài 1. Sắp xếp các câu A-D để hoàn thành hội thoại giữa Kevin và Ly nha. <eng>Put the sentences (A-D) in order to complete the conversation.</eng> 1 phút.
</teacher_script>

<task>
**Đề:** Put the sentences (A-D) in order to complete the conversation. Then practise it in pairs.
**Dịch đề:** Sắp xếp câu A-D để hoàn thành hội thoại.
</task>

<questions type="ordering">
A. Yes. My grandparents are over 60 now. They hold very traditional views about everything.
B. I see. What about your parents?
C. Yes, I do. I live with my grandparents, my parents, and my younger sister.
D. That's interesting. Have you noticed any differences between the generations?
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Ok đáp án nè.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Đáp án:** 1-C | 2-D | 3-A | 4-B

**Kevin:** Ly, do you live with your extended family?
**Ly:** (1) **C** - Yes, I do. I live with my grandparents, my parents, and my younger sister.
**Kevin:** (2) **D** - That's interesting. Have you noticed any differences between the generations?
**Ly:** (3) **A** - Yes. My grandparents are over 60 now. They hold very traditional views about everything.
**Kevin:** (4) **B** - I see. What about your parents?
**Ly:** Well, they're only in their 40s. I think they're quite open to new ways of thinking. I can share lots of things with my parents.
</answer>

<explanation>
Cách bắt đầu cuộc trò chuyện về chủ đề thế hệ:
1. Hỏi thông tin cá nhân: "Do you live with your extended family?"
2. Thể hiện quan tâm + hỏi sâu hơn: "That's interesting. Have you noticed any differences?"
3. Đưa ví dụ cụ thể: "My grandparents hold traditional views..."
4. Hỏi tiếp: "What about your parents?"
</explanation>

### Bài 2 trang 26 - Talk about generations

<teacher_script pause="120" action="record">
Bài 2. Nói về các thế hệ khác nhau trong gia đình em. Dùng các ý tưởng gợi ý nha. <eng>Talk about the different generations of your family.</eng> Ghi âm lại. 2 phút.
</teacher_script>

<task>
**Đề:** Work in pairs. Talk about the different generations of your family. Use the model and tips in 1, and the ideas below.
**Dịch đề:** Nói về các thế hệ khác nhau trong gia đình bạn.

**Suggested ideas:**
- be open to new ways of thinking *(cởi mở với cách suy nghĩ mới)*
- have fixed ideas about the world *(có quan niệm cố định)*
- want to make their own decisions *(muốn tự quyết định)*
- hold traditional views about many issues *(giữ quan điểm truyền thống)*
- not want big changes in life *(không muốn thay đổi lớn)*
- be good at using electronic devices *(giỏi dùng thiết bị điện tử)*
</task>
