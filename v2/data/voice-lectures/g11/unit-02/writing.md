### Bài 2 trang 28 - Read opinion essay

<teacher_script pause="0">
Bài 2. Đọc bài luận mẫu rồi nối mỗi đoạn với mô tả đúng nha. <eng>Read an opinion essay and match each paragraph with the correct description.</eng>
</teacher_script>

<task>
**Đề:** Read an opinion essay and match each paragraph with the correct description.
**Dịch đề:** Đọc bài luận và nối từng đoạn với mô tả chính xác.
</task>

<reading>
**Should parents strictly limit teenagers' screen time?**

**Paragraph 1:** Many teenagers nowadays spend too much time on digital devices. I firmly believe that parents should strictly limit their screen time for two reasons.

**Paragraph 2:** First of all, teenagers who spend a lot of time on screens are less likely to finish their homework. If parents limit their screen time, teenagers will have more time not only for learning, but also for outdoor activities.

**Paragraph 3:** In addition, too much screen time is bad for teenagers' health. Looking at a computer or smartphone screen for a long time can damage their eyesight and cause headaches. Too much screen time may also lead to sleep and weight problems.

**Paragraph 4:** In conclusion, I think that parents need to control the time their teenage children spend on digital devices. This will ensure that their children have time for homework and outdoor activities, and will protect children's health.
</reading>

<questions type="matching">
A. Second reason with examples and facts
B. Conclusion summarising the writer's point of view
C. First reason with examples and facts
D. Introduction to the issue and the writer's point of view
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="0" href="audio/tts_29c83a10.mp3">
Ok đáp án nè.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Đáp án:** Paragraph 1-D | Paragraph 2-C | Paragraph 3-A | Paragraph 4-B
</answer>

<explanation>
Cấu trúc bài luận nêu ý kiến (opinion essay):
1. **Introduction (D)** - Giới thiệu vấn đề + nêu quan điểm
2. **Body 1 (C)** - Lý do 1 + ví dụ/dẫn chứng (First of all,...)
3. **Body 2 (A)** - Lý do 2 + ví dụ/dẫn chứng (In addition,...)
4. **Conclusion (B)** - Kết luận tóm tắt quan điểm (In conclusion,...)
</explanation>

<grammar>
**Cấu trúc bài luận nêu ý kiến:**
- **Introduction:** I firmly/strongly believe that... for two reasons.
- **Body 1:** First of all,... (lý do 1 + ví dụ)
- **Body 2:** In addition,... (lý do 2 + ví dụ)
- **Conclusion:** In conclusion, I think that...

**Useful phrases:**
- I firmly/strongly believe that...
- First of all, / Firstly, / To begin with,
- In addition, / Moreover, / Furthermore,
- In conclusion, / To sum up, / All in all,
</grammar>

### Bài 3 trang 29 - Write opposite view

<teacher_script pause="300">
Bài 3. Viết bài luận 120-150 từ nêu quan điểm NGƯỢC LẠI nha - tức là bố mẹ KHÔNG NÊN giới hạn nghiêm ngặt thời gian dùng thiết bị. <eng>Write an opinion essay (120-150 words) stating the opposite view.</eng> 5 phút.
</teacher_script>

<task>
**Đề:** Write an opinion essay (120-150 words) stating the opposite view. Start with: "Digital devices play an essential part in teenagers' lives nowadays. I strongly believe that parents should not strictly limit their children's screen time for the following reasons."
**Dịch đề:** Viết bài luận (120-150 từ) nêu quan điểm ngược lại.
</task>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Ok bài mẫu nè.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Bài mẫu:**

Digital devices play an essential part in teenagers' lives nowadays. I strongly believe that parents should not strictly limit their children's screen time for the following reasons.

First of all, I think that by limiting the use of technology, parents will also limit what teens can benefit from it. In fact, teenagers can learn a lot of useful knowledge and necessary skills through online educational games and videos.

In addition, when teenagers' screen time is strictly controlled by their parents, the gap between parents and children may become wider. Teenagers may think that their parents are very mean and not fair to them, and may refuse to talk to them.

In conclusion, I believe that it is not a good idea for parents to strictly limit their teenagers' screen time. Teens can benefit from screen activities that encourage learning and parents can develop a better relationship with their children.
</answer>
