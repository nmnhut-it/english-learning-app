<teacher_script pause="0">

### Grammar

<teacher_script pause="60">
Phần <eng>Grammar</eng>. Viết lại câu dùng <eng>perfect participle clauses</eng> hoặc <eng>perfect gerunds</eng>. 1 phút nha.

<task>
**Đề:** Rewrite these sentences using perfect participle clauses or perfect gerunds.
**Dịch đề:** Viết lại câu sử dụng mệnh đề phân từ hoàn thành hoặc danh động từ hoàn thành.
</task>

<questions type="complete_sentences">
1. After he finished school, my brother took a year off and travelled around the world.
→ _______ took a year off and travelled around the world.
2. He did not remember that he had discussed his study options with his parents.
→ _______ his study options with his parents.
3. My cousin didn't ask anyone for advice, so she made the wrong decision about her education.
→ _______ made the wrong decision about her education.
4. Lan won the first prize in the competition. This is something she's very proud of.
→ _______ is something Lan is very proud of.
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Đáp án nè.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Đáp án:**
1. **Having finished school, my brother** took a year off and travelled around the world.
2. **He didn't remember having discussed** his study options with his parents.
3. **Not having asked anyone for advice, my cousin** made the wrong decision about her education.
4. **Having won the first prize in the competition** is something Lan is very proud of.
</answer>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Câu 1: <eng>After he finished</eng> → <eng>Having finished</eng>. Mệnh đề phân từ hoàn thành, hành động xong trước rồi mới đi du lịch.
</teacher_script>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Câu 2: <eng>did not remember that he had discussed</eng> → <eng>didn't remember having discussed</eng>. Danh động từ hoàn thành sau <eng>remember</eng>.
</teacher_script>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Câu 3: <eng>didn't ask</eng> → <eng>Not having asked</eng>. Phủ định: đặt <eng>Not</eng> trước <eng>having</eng>. Không hỏi ai nên quyết định sai.
</teacher_script>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Câu 4: <eng>Lan won</eng> → <eng>Having won</eng>. Dùng <eng>Having + V3</eng> làm chủ ngữ (danh động từ hoàn thành). Giành giải nhất là điều Lan tự hào.
</teacher_script>

<explanation>
**Tổng kết Having + V3:**

| Cách dùng | Ví dụ | Ghi nhớ |
|-----------|-------|---------|
| Mệnh đề phân từ hoàn thành | **Having finished** school, he travelled. | Hành động trước → hành động sau |
| Phủ định | **Not having asked** anyone, she failed. | Not + having + V3 |
| Danh động từ hoàn thành (sau V) | He regretted **having discussed** it. | Sau remember/forget/regret/deny |
| Danh động từ hoàn thành (chủ ngữ) | **Having won** the prize is great. | Having + V3 làm chủ ngữ |
</explanation>
