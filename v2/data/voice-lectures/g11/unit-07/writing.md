### Bài 1 - Complete the enquiries

<teacher_script pause="60">
Bài 1, xem quảng cáo về trường nghề SGV và khóa đào tạo hướng dẫn viên du lịch. Hoàn thành các câu hỏi. 1 phút nha.
</teacher_script>

<task>
**Đề:** Look at the advertisement about a vocational school and its tour guide training courses. You want to ask for more information. Complete the enquiries.
**Dịch đề:** Xem quảng cáo về trường dạy nghề và khóa đào tạo hướng dẫn viên du lịch. Bạn muốn hỏi thêm thông tin. Hoàn thành các câu hỏi.
</task>

<reading>
**SGV Vocational School**
- Email: sgvvocational@gmail.com
- Address: 100 New Street, Binh An City

**Want to become a tour guide?**
- Are you over 18 and love travelling and exploring different cultures?
- Apply to SGV Vocational School. We offer tour guide training courses all year round.
  + No qualifications needed – Do you need to do a test?
  + Low cost – Discounts for disadvantaged students?
  + Apprenticeships available – Weekly wages?
</reading>

<questions type="fill_blanks">
1. Could you please tell me _______?
2. I would like to know _______.
3. I would appreciate it if you could tell me _______.
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Đáp án nè.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Đáp án:**
1. Could you please tell me **if there is a test requirement to enroll in the tour guide training courses**?
2. I would like to know **if SGV Vocational School offers any discounts for students who are financially disadvantaged**.
3. I would appreciate it if you could tell me **the weekly wage for the apprenticeships provided by SGV Vocational School**.
</answer>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Câu 1 hỏi về bài kiểm tra đầu vào: <eng>if there is a test requirement</eng>. Câu 2 hỏi về giảm giá cho học sinh khó khăn: <eng>if... offers any discounts</eng>. Câu 3 hỏi về lương học nghề: <eng>the weekly wage for the apprenticeships</eng>.
</teacher_script>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Chú ý các cấu trúc hỏi lịch sự nha: <eng>Could you please tell me...</eng>, <eng>I would like to know...</eng>, <eng>I would appreciate it if you could tell me...</eng>. Dùng trong thư trang trọng.
</teacher_script>

<!-- chunk: exercise_2 -->
### Bài 2 - Write a request letter

<teacher_script pause="300">
Bài 2, viết thư yêu cầu thông tin về khóa học ở bài 1. Khoảng 140-170 từ. Dùng dàn bài gợi ý. 5 phút nha.
</teacher_script>

<task>
**Đề:** Write a letter (140-170 words) to request information about the courses in 1.
**Dịch đề:** Viết thư (140-170 từ) yêu cầu thông tin về các khóa học trong phần 1.
</task>

<reading>
**Outline - Dàn bài:**

Dear Sir or Madam,

I am writing to ask for more information about _______

First, I would appreciate it if you could tell me _______

Next, I would like to know _______

Finally, it would be great if you _______

I look forward to hearing from you.

Yours faithfully,
</reading>

<teacher_script pause="0" action="photo">
Viết xong chụp hình gửi nha.
</teacher_script>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Đây là bài mẫu để tham khảo nha.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Bài mẫu:**

Dear Sir or Madam,

I am writing to enquire about the tour guide training courses offered by SGV Vocational School. I am very interested in exploring different cultures and would like to know more about the courses.

Firstly, I would like to ask if there is a test requirement to enroll in the tour guide training courses. As I do not have any previous qualifications in this field, I would like to know if there are any specific requirements for admission.

Secondly, I would appreciate it if you could inform me if SGV Vocational School offers any discounts for students who are financially disadvantaged. This would greatly assist me in planning for the cost of the training.

Lastly, I would like to enquire about the weekly wage for the apprenticeships provided by SGV Vocational School. It would be great if you could provide me with more information about this opportunity.

Thank you for considering my request. I look forward to hearing from you soon.

Yours faithfully,
Lan
</answer>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Nhớ cấu trúc thư yêu cầu nha: Mở bài nêu lý do viết thư. Thân bài hỏi từng câu hỏi cụ thể, dùng cấu trúc lịch sự. Kết bài: <eng>I look forward to hearing from you</eng>. Ký tên: <eng>Yours faithfully</eng> vì không biết tên người nhận.
</teacher_script>
