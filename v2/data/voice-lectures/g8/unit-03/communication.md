<reading>
### Ann
Ann uses YouTube every day. She loves to upload her own videos and watch other people's videos. She says she learns a lot from the videos she watches, and she also enjoys the comments she gets on her own videos.

### Tom
Tom is on Facebook every day. He connects with his friends, posts photos, and shares posts he finds interesting. He thinks Facebook is the most user-friendly platform for staying in touch with people his age.

### Trang
Trang loves Instagram. She says it's really fun and easy to browse. She likes seeing how many likes her photos get, and she checks her notifications often to see if people have commented on her pictures.

### Nam
Nam has a Facebook page, but he doesn't update it very frequently. He mainly uses Facebook Messenger to chat with his classmates and discuss studying. He says it is the most convenient way to organise group study sessions.
</reading>

<answer>
**Đáp án:**
1. Trang
2. Ann
3. Nam
4. Tom
5. Tom
</answer>

<explanation>
**1. Trang** — Trang *"checks her notifications often"* → thường xuyên kiểm tra thông báo. Đặc điểm nổi bật của Trang là để ý lượt thích và bình luận.

**2. Ann** — Ann *"loves to upload her own videos and watch other people's videos"* → xem video trên YouTube là hoạt động chính của Ann.

**3. Nam** — Nam *"mainly uses Facebook Messenger to chat with his classmates and discuss studying"* → dùng Messenger để thảo luận việc học. Nam khác với 3 bạn kia — dùng MXH chủ yếu cho việc học.

**4. Tom** — Tom *"thinks Facebook is the most user-friendly platform"* → nghĩ Facebook thân thiện với người dùng.

**5. Tom** — Tom *"connects with his friends"* trên Facebook → câu 4 và 5 đều là Tom vì cả hai đặc điểm này đều được nhắc đến trong đoạn văn về Tom.
</explanation>

<task>
**Đề:** Work in pairs. Ask your partner about their social media habits. Use the questions below.
**Dịch đề:** Làm việc theo cặp. Hỏi bạn của bạn về thói quen mạng xã hội. Dùng các câu hỏi bên dưới.

**Câu hỏi gợi ý:**
1. What social media platforms do you use?
2. How often do you use them? (every day / a few times a week / rarely)
3. What do you usually do on social media? (upload photos / browse / chat / check notifications...)
4. Why do you like using that platform?
5. Do you think social media is good or bad for teenagers? Why?
</task>
