**1.** Polluted water is unsafe for (drinking / cooking).
**2.** Sometimes toxic substances flow into rivers from (factories / hospitals).
**3.** Water pollution has a (dangerous / harmful) effect on our life.
**4.** We (couldn't / shouldn't) throw litter into rivers and lakes.

2. from **factories**.
3. **harmful** effect.
4. **shouldn't** throw litter.

<questions type="open_ended">
**1.** What is the listening text about?
**2.** How many sources of water pollution are there?
**3.** What are two common sources of drinking water?
**4.** What type of effect does water pollution have on our life?
**5.** What products can we use to reduce water pollution?
</questions>

<answer>
**Đáp án:**
1. Water pollution (Ô nhiễm nước).
2. Two (Hai nguồn: man-made and natural).
3. Rivers and lakes (Sông và hồ).
4. Harmful effect (Ảnh hưởng có hại).
5. Green products (Sản phẩm xanh).
</answer>

### Bài 5 trang 79 - Write a notice

<teacher_script pause="180" href="audio/tts_d5b1d729.mp3">

<task>

- **Who:** Go Green Club
- **What:** Lecture on Water Pollution

- **Where:** School Grand Hall
- **Topic:** Water Pollution
</task>

<dialogue>
**Sample Notice:**

**Go Green Club**
**NOTICE**
Feb 20th

**Lecture on Water Pollution**

The Go Green Club is organising a lecture on water pollution. Students from all classes are welcome to attend.

**Details:**
- Time: 2 p.m. - 4 p.m., 6 March
- Place: School Grand Hall
- Topic: Water Pollution

If you have questions, please contact the club leader.

**Club Leader**
(Signed)
</dialogue>
