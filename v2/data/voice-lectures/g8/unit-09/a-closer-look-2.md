<!-- chunk: grammar -->
### PAST CONTINUOUS (Thi Qua khu tiep dien)

<teacher_script pause="0">
Gio hoc ngu phap nha. Thi qua khu tiep dien dung de noi ve hanh dong dang dien ra tai mot thoi diem trong qua khu.
</teacher_script>

<grammar>
**Thi qua khu tiep dien (Past Continuous)**

**1. Cau truc:**
- **(+)** S + **was/were** + V-**ing**
- **(-)** S + **was/were** + **not** + V-**ing**
- **(?)** **Was/Were** + S + V-**ing**?

**2. Cach dung:**
Dien ta mot hanh dong **dang dien ra** tai mot thoi diem nhat dinh trong qua khu.

**3. Dau hieu nhan biet:**
- at + gio + yesterday/last night (at 9 o'clock yesterday)
- when + menh de qua khu don
- while + menh de qua khu tiep dien

**4. Quy tac quan trong:**
Hanh dong **DAI** (dang dien ra) → qua khu tiep dien
Hanh dong **NGAN** (cat ngang) → qua khu don

**Vi du:** When I **was sleeping** (dai), the earthquake **hit** (ngan).
</grammar>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Meo nho ne: Nhu phim dang chay thi ai do bam pause - phim dang chay la qua khu tiep dien, bam pause la qua khu don.
</teacher_script>

### Bai tap bo sung - Past Continuous

<teacher_script pause="120">
Truoc khi lam bai trong sach, lam bai tap bo sung de luyen tap thi qua khu tiep dien nha. Chia dong tu trong ngoac. 2 phut.
</teacher_script>

<task>
**De:** Chia dong tu trong ngoac dung thi (qua khu tiep dien hoac qua khu don).
**Dich de:** Put the verbs in brackets into the correct tense (past continuous or past simple).
</task>

<questions type="fill_blanks">
1. People _______ (sleep) in their houses when the earthquake _______ (hit).
   *(Moi nguoi _______ trong nha khi dong dat _______.)*

2. It _______ (rain) heavily when the flood _______ (start).
   *(Troi _______ lon khi lu _______.)*

3. My family _______ (watch) TV when the storm _______ (come).
   *(Gia dinh toi _______ TV khi bao _______.)*

4. The police _______ (help) people when the second earthquake _______ (happen).
   *(Canh sat _______ nguoi dan khi tran dong dat thu hai _______.)*

5. While we _______ (stay) at home, the strong wind _______ (break) our windows.
   *(Trong khi chung toi _______ o nha, gio manh _______ cua so.)*

6. They _______ (run) away when the fire _______ (spread).
   *(Ho _______ di khi lua _______.)*

7. I _______ (walk) to school when the rain _______ (begin).
   *(Toi _______ den truong khi mua _______.)*

8. All the students _______ (study) when they _______ (feel) the ground shake.
   *(Tat ca hoc sinh _______ khi ho _______ mat dat rung.)*

9. The children _______ (play) outside when the storm clouds _______ (appear).
   *(Bon tre _______ ngoai troi khi may bao _______.)*

10. We _______ (drive) on the road when the trees _______ (fall) down.
    *(Chung toi _______ tren duong khi cay _______.)*
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Ok dap an ne.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Dap an:**
1. **were sleeping** / **hit**
2. **was raining** / **started**
3. **was watching** / **came**
4. **were helping** / **happened**
5. **were staying** / **broke**
6. **were running** / **spread**
7. **was walking** / **began**
8. **were studying** / **felt**
9. **were playing** / **appeared**
10. **were driving** / **fell**

**Giai thich:** Hanh dong dang dien ra (dai) dung qua khu tiep dien, hanh dong cat ngang (ngan) dung qua khu don.
</answer>

### Bai 1 trang 95 - Past Continuous

<teacher_script pause="90">
Bai 1. Hoan thanh cau bang cach chia dong tu trong ngoac o thi qua khu tiep dien. 90 giay.
</teacher_script>

<task>
**De:** Complete the sentences by putting the verbs in brackets into the past continuous.
**Dich de:** Hoan thanh cau bang cach chia dong tu trong ngoac o thi qua khu tiep dien.
</task>

<questions type="fill_blanks">
1. They (help) _______ the flood victims at 10 o'clock last night.
   *(Ho _______ nhung nan nhan lu lut luc 10 gio dem qua.)*

2. It (not snow) _______ when I left home this afternoon.
   *(Troi _______ khi toi roi nha chieu nay.)*

3. While she (work) _______ the field, the tornado came.
   *(Trong khi co ay _______ tren canh dong, loc xoay den.)*

4. - What _______ you (do) _______ at 9 o'clock yesterday morning?
   - I (watch) _______ the news about a volcanic eruption.
   *(- Cau _______ gi luc 9 gio sang hom qua?)*
   *(- Minh _______ tin tuc ve nui lua phun trao.)*

5. - _______ you (cry) _______ when I saw you two days ago?
   - No, I wasn't.
   *(- Cau co _______ khi minh thay cau hai ngay truoc khong?)*
   *(- Khong.)*
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Ok dap an ne.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Dap an:**
1. They **were helping** the flood victims at 10 o'clock last night.
2. It **wasn't snowing** when I left home this afternoon.
3. While she **was working** the field, the tornado came.
4. - What **were** you **doing** at 9 o'clock yesterday morning?
   - I **was watching** the news about a volcanic eruption.
5. - **Were** you **crying** when I saw you two days ago?
   - No, I wasn't.

**Giai thich:**
- Cau 1, 4, 5: co thoi diem cu the (at 10 o'clock, at 9 o'clock) → qua khu tiep dien
- Cau 2: hanh dong dang dien ra khi hanh dong khac xay ra (when I left)
- Cau 3: co **while** → qua khu tiep dien
</answer>

### Bai 2 trang 95 - Choose the correct form

<teacher_script pause="90">
Bai 2. Khoanh tron dap an dung. Bai nay kho hon vi em phai phan biet qua khu don va qua khu tiep dien. 90 giay.
</teacher_script>

<task>
**De:** Circle the correct answer to complete each sentence.
**Dich de:** Khoanh tron dap an dung de hoan thanh moi cau.
</task>

<questions type="multiple_choice">
**1.** We **donated / were donating** money to help the earthquake victims last month.
*(Chung toi _______ tien de giup nan nhan dong dat thang truoc.)*

**2.** - What **did you do / were you doing** when the volcano erupted?
    - I **slept / was sleeping** in my bed.
*(- Cau _______ gi khi nui lua phun trao?)*
*(- Minh _______ tren giuong.)*

**3.** While they **camped / were camping** near the river, the flood **was coming / came** suddenly.
*(Trong khi ho _______ gan song, lu _______ bat ngo.)*

**4.** After Tom **ran / was running** out of his house, he **moved / was moving** quickly to a safer place.
*(Sau khi Tom _______ ra khoi nha, anh ay _______ nhanh chong den noi an toan hon.)*

**5.** I **didn't read / wasn't reading** a newspaper at 9 a.m. yesterday, but I **watched / was watching** the news about the tornado.
*(Minh _______ bao luc 9 gio sang hom qua, nhung minh _______ tin ve loc xoay.)*
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Ok dap an ne.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Dap an:** 1. donated | 2. were you doing - was sleeping | 3. were camping - came | 4. ran - moved | 5. wasn't reading - watched

**Giai thich chi tiet:**

**1. donated** - Hanh dong hoan thanh trong qua khu (last month) → qua khu don

**2. were you doing - was sleeping** - Hanh dong dang dien ra khi nui lua phun (cat ngang) → qua khu tiep dien

**3. were camping - came** - Dang cam trai (dai, qua khu tiep dien) thi lu den (ngan, qua khu don)

**4. ran - moved** - Hai hanh dong lien tiep xay ra va ket thuc trong qua khu → deu dung qua khu don

**5. wasn't reading - watched** - Tai 9 a.m. (thoi diem cu the) → qua khu tiep dien. Xem tin la hanh dong hoan thanh → qua khu don
</answer>

### Bai 3 trang 95 - Describe the picture

<teacher_script pause="90">
Bai 3. Nhin tranh va viet cau mo ta moi nguoi trong gia dinh Lan dang lam gi khi dong dat xay ra. Dung cac tu trong hop. 90 giay.
</teacher_script>

<task>
**De:** Look at the picture and write what each person in Lan's family was doing when the earthquake happened. Use the given words and phrases from the box.
**Dich de:** Nhin vao buc tranh va viet nhung gi moi nguoi trong gia dinh Lan dang lam khi tran dong dat xay ra. Su dung cac tu va cum tu trong hop.
</task>

<questions type="fill_blanks">
**Tu de dung:**
| watch TV | draw | read a book | drink tea | talk on the phone |

*(Nhin tranh gia dinh Lan va viet cau)*
1. Lan's mother _______ while Lan's grandparents _______.
2. Lan's father _______ while he _______.
3. Lan's family _______ when she _______.
4. Lan's brother _______ when she _______.
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Ok dap an ne.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Dap an:**
1. Lan's mother **was reading a book** while Lan's grandparents **were watching TV**.
   *(Me Lan dang doc sach trong khi ong ba dang xem TV.)*

2. Lan's father **was drinking tea** while he **was watching TV**.
   *(Bo Lan dang uong tra trong khi dang xem TV.)*

3. Lan's family **was watching TV** when she **was talking on the phone**.
   *(Gia dinh Lan dang xem TV khi co ay dang noi chuyen dien thoai.)*

4. Lan's brother **was drawing** when she **was talking on the phone**.
   *(Em trai Lan dang ve khi co ay dang noi chuyen dien thoai.)*

**Quy tac:** Khi hai hanh dong xay ra dong thoi trong qua khu, ca hai deu dung qua khu tiep dien. **While** va **when** o day noi hai hanh dong song song.
</answer>

### Bai 4 trang 95 - Form questions

<teacher_script pause="90">
Bai 4. Tao cau hoi su dung thi qua khu tiep dien. Hoi va tra loi ve nhung gi em dang lam vao cac thoi diem toi qua. 90 giay.
</teacher_script>

<task>
**De:** Form questions using the past continuous. Then in pairs, ask and answer the questions.
**Dich de:** Tao cau hoi su dung thi qua khu tiep dien. Sau do theo cap, hoi va tra loi cac cau hoi.
</task>

<questions type="speaking">
**Example:**
you / play / football / 5 o'clock yesterday afternoon?
→ **Were you playing football at 5 o'clock yesterday afternoon?**
- Yes, I was. / No, I wasn't. I was doing my homework.

**Form questions:**
1. you / have dinner / 7 o'clock yesterday evening?
2. you / do / homework / 8 o'clock yesterday evening?
3. you / watch / film / 9 o'clock yesterday evening?
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Ok dap an ne.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Dap an:**

**1.** Were you having dinner at 7 o'clock yesterday evening?
*(Cau co dang an toi luc 7 gio toi qua khong?)*
- Yes, I was. / No, I wasn't. I was playing piano.
*(Co. / Khong, minh dang choi dan.)*

**2.** Were you doing homework at 8 o'clock yesterday evening?
*(Cau co dang lam bai tap luc 8 gio toi qua khong?)*
- Yes, I was. / No, I wasn't. I was watching TV.
*(Co. / Khong, minh dang xem TV.)*

**3.** Were you watching film at 9 o'clock yesterday evening?
*(Cau co dang xem phim luc 9 gio toi qua khong?)*
- Yes, I was. / No, I wasn't. I was sleeping.
*(Co. / Khong, minh dang ngu.)*

**Cau truc cau hoi:** **Were** + you + V-**ing** + at + gio + yesterday?
</answer>
