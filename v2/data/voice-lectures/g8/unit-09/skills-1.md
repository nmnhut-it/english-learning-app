### Bai 1 trang 97 - Matching

<task>
**De:** Match the headlines (1 - 2) with the natural disasters (A - B).
**Dich de:** Ghep cac tieu de (1 - 2) voi cac tham hoa thien nhien (A - B).
</task>

<questions type="matching">
**Headlines:**
1. 30 seconds of a slight shaking in Hanoi
   *(30 giay rung lac nhe o Ha Noi)*

2. A thick layer of ash covers Tonga
   *(Mot lop tro day phu Tonga)*

**Natural disasters:**
- A. volcanic eruption *(nui lua phun trao)*
- B. earthquake *(dong dat)*
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Ok dap an ne.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Dap an:** 1 - B | 2 - A

**Giai thich:**
- **1 - B**: 30 seconds of a slight shaking in Hanoi - earthquake
  *(30 giay rung lac nhe o Ha Noi - dong dat)*
  → Rung lac la dau hieu cua dong dat

- **2 - A**: A thick layer of ash covers Tonga - volcanic eruption
  *(Mot lop tro day phu Tonga - nui lua phun trao)*
  → Tro bui la dau hieu cua nui lua phun trao
</answer>

### Bai 3 trang 97 - Answer the questions

<task>
**De:** Read the articles again and answer the questions.

<questions type="fill_blanks">
1. Where and when did the eruption happen?
   *(Vu phun trao xay ra o dau va khi nao?)*

2. What did the eruption cause?
   *(Vu phun trao gay ra cai gi?)*

3. What were the other effects of the eruption?
   *(Nhung anh huong khac cua vu phun trao la gi?)*

4. How long did the buildings shake?
   *(Cac toa nha lac trong bao lau?)*

5. What caused the shaking?
   *(Dieu gi gay ra su rung lac?)*
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Ok dap an ne.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Dap an:**

1. A volcano in the South Pacific erupted violently last Saturday.
   *(Mot ngon nui o phia nam Thai Binh Duong da phun trao rat du doi vao thu Bay tuan truoc.)*

2. The eruption also caused a tsunami which flooded properties in Tonga's capital.
   *(Vu phun trao cung gay ra mot con song than lam ngap lut tai san o thu do cua Tonga.)*

3. It destroyed hundreds of homes on some small islands. More than twenty people on these islands are still missing.
   *(No da pha huy hang tram ngoi nha tren nhung hon dao nho. Hon hai muoi nguoi tren dao van dang mat tich.)*

4. The buildings shake for about 30 seconds.
   *(Cac toa nha da lac trong 30 giay.)*

5. A strong earthquake in China caused this shaking.
   *(Mot tran dong dat manh o Trung Quoc gay ra su rung lac nay.)*
</answer>

**Questions:**

   *(Do la mot tran lu nghiem trong.)*

### Bai 5 trang 97 - Speaking Practice

<task>
**De:** Work in groups. Prepare a short piece of news about the natural disaster in 4 or one you know of. Report the news to the class.
**Dich de:** Lam viec nhom. Soan mot doan tin ngan ve tham hoa thien nhien o bai 4 hoac mot tin tuc ma em biet. Bao cao tin tuc cho ca lop.
</task>

**Example:** *(Vi du)*

Five days of heavy rain caused a serious flood in a village in Phu Yen. The flood happened last week. ...

*(Nam ngay mua to da gay ra mot tran lut nghiem trong tai mot ngoi lang o Phu Yen. ...)*

**Sample answer:**

Five days of heavy rain caused a serious flood in a village in Phu Yen last week. Many houses were flooded in the disaster. Sadly, two villagers died, and five others were missing. Rescue workers came to look for the missing people. Meanwhile, volunteers helped by giving food and drinks to the victims. The local government is working to help people rebuild their homes after this disaster.

*(Nam ngay mua to da gay ra mot tran lut nghiem trong tai mot ngoi lang o Phu Yen tuan truoc. Nhieu ngoi nha bi ngap lut trong tham hoa. Dang buon la hai dan lang da chet, va nam nguoi khac mat tich. Nhan vien cuu ho da den tim nhung nguoi mat tich. Trong khi do, tinh nguyen vien giup do bang cach dua thuc an va nuoc uong cho cac nan nhan. Chinh quyen dia phuong dang lam viec de giup nguoi dan xay dung lai nha cua sau tham hoa.)*

Bam nut ghi am va noi lai doan van. Khi noi, em dung thi qua khu don vi su kien da xay ra. Vi du: <eng>Five days of heavy rain caused...</eng>, <eng>Two villagers died...</eng>
