### Bai 2 trang 88 - Reading Comprehension

<teacher_script pause="0">
Bai 2 <eng>Read the passage and choose</eng>. Doc bai van ve ly do nguoi ta di trung tam mua sam nha. Khong chi de mua sam dau ma con nhieu ly do khac nua!
</teacher_script>

<reading>
**Why We Go to Shopping Centres**

| English | Vietnamese |
|---------|------------|
| Shopping centres attract a lot of customers, especially at the weekend, on holidays, or during sales. | Trung tam thuong mai thu hut rat nhieu khach hang, dac biet la vao cuoi tuan, vao ngay nghi hoac trong cac dot giam gia. |
| People go there to shop. | Moi nguoi den do de mua hang. |
| Shopping centres offer a wide range of products to choose from. | Trung tam thuong mai cung cap da dang hang hoa de lua chon. |
| Customers can touch the products and try on clothes and shoes. | Khach hang co the cam san pham va thu quan ao hay giay dep. |
| This makes them feel more comfortable when they decide to buy something. | Chuyen nay khien ho thay thoai mai hon khi ho quyet dinh mua gi do. |
| However, people also go to shopping centres for many other reasons. | Tuy nhien, nguoi ta cung di den trung tam thuong mai vi nhieu li do khac. |
| Some people go there for entertainment. | Nhieu nguoi den do de giai tri. |
| These centres often offer year-round free entertainment for customers of all ages such as live music and special performances. | Nhung trung tam nay thuong cung cap nhung dich vu giai tri mien phi quanh nam cho cac khach hang o moi do tuoi nhu nhac song va nhung man trinh dien dac biet. |
| During holidays, shoppers can see decorations and join in the holiday excitement. | Trong nhung ngay le, khach mua hang co the thay nhung vat trang tri va hoa minh vao niem thich thu cua nhung ngay le. |
| It's a good way to relax. | Do la mot cach tot de thu gian. |
| Some people go there just to hang out with friends. | Vai nguoi den do chi de di choi voi ban be. |
| They go browsing and chat while wandering through the shopping malls. | Ho chi di luot qua va noi chuyen voi nhau trong khi lang thang khap trung tam thuong mai. |
| Others visit shopping centres to get exercise. | Co nhung nguoi den trung tam thuong mai chi de tap the duc. |
| They enjoy walking for one or two hours in clean and well-lit areas. | Ho thich di dao tam mot den hai gio dong ho trong nhung khu vuc sach se va sang den. |
| Some people even go there to avoid the heat or cold outside. | Co vai nguoi tham chi con den day de tranh cai nong va cai lanh o ben ngoai. |
| Shopping centres offer free air conditioning and heating. | Trung tam thuong mai cung cap suoi am va lam mat mien phi. |
</reading>

<teacher_script pause="180">
Doc bai van roi tra loi 5 cau hoi trac nghiem nha. 3 phut.
</teacher_script>

<task>
**De:** Read the passage and choose the correct answer A, B or C.
**Dich de:** Doc doan van chon cau tra loi dung A, B hoac C.
</task>

<questions type="multiple_choice">
**1.** People go to a shopping centre _______.
- A. only for shopping
- B. mainly to hang out
- C. for many reasons
*Moi nguoi den trung tam mua sam _______.*
*A. chi de mua sam | B. chu yeu de di choi | C. vi nhieu ly do*

**2.** At shopping centres, customers can _______.
- A. touch the products
- B. bargain to get lower prices
- C. pay to watch live performances
*Tai cac trung tam mua sam, khach hang co the _______.*
*A. cham vao san pham | B. mac ca de co duoc gia thap hon | C. tra tien de xem bieu dien truc tiep*

**3.** A kind of entertainment at a shopping centre is _______.
- A. fashion contests
- B. live music
- C. painting exhibitions
*Mot loai hinh giai tri tai trung tam mua sam la _______.*
*A. cuoc thi thoi trang | B. nhac song | C. trien lam tranh*

**4.** Some people exercise at a shopping centre because they like its _______.
- A. crowds
- B. well-lit areas
- C. shops
*Mot so nguoi tap the duc tai mot trung tam mua sam boi vi ho thich _______ cua no.*
*A. dam dong | B. khu vuc co nhieu anh sang | C. cua hang*

**5.** The word "year-round" in paragraph 2 is similar in meaning to _______.
- A. always
- B. sometimes
- C. rarely
*Tu "year-round" trong doan 2 co nghia tuong tu nhu _______.*
*A. luon luon | B. thinh thoang | C. hiem khi*
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Ok dap an ne.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Dap an:** 1.C | 2.A | 3.B | 4.B | 5.A
</answer>

<explanation>
**Giai thich chi tiet:**

**1. C - for many reasons**
People go to a shopping centre **for many reasons**.
*(Moi nguoi den trung tam thuong mai vi nhieu li do.)*
**Thong tin:** However, people also go to shopping centres for many other reasons.
*(Tuy nhien, nguoi ta cung di den trung tam thuong mai vi nhieu li do khac.)*

**2. A - touch the products**
At shopping centres, customers can **touch the products**.
*(O trung tam thuong mai, khach hang co the cham vao san pham.)*
**Thong tin:** Customers can touch the products and try on clothes and shoes.
*(Khach hang co the cam san pham va thu quan ao hay giay dep.)*

**3. B - live music**
A kind of entertainment at a shopping centre is **live music**.
*(Mot kieu giai tri o trung tam thuong mai la nhac song.)*
**Thong tin:** These centres often offer year-round free entertainment for customers of all ages such as live music and special performances.
*(Nhung trung tam nay thuong cung cap nhung dich vu giai tri mien phi quanh nam cho cac khach hang o moi do tuoi nhu nhac song va nhung man trinh dien dac biet.)*

**4. B - well-lit areas**
Some people exercise at a shopping centre because they like its **well-lit areas**.
*(Vai nguoi thich tap the duc vi ho thich nhung khu vuc day du anh sang cua trung tam mua sam.)*
**Thong tin:** Others visit shopping centres to get exercise. They enjoy walking for one or two hours in clean and well-lit areas.
*(Co nhung nguoi den trung tam thuong mai chi de tap the duc. Ho thich di dao tam mot den hai gio dong ho trong nhung khu vuc sach se va sang den.)*

**5. A - always**
The word "year-round" in paragraph 2 is similar in meaning to **always**.
*(Tu "quanh nam" trong doan so 2 co cung nghia voi tu luon luon.)*
**Thong tin:** These centres often offer year-round free entertainment for customers of all ages such as live music and special performances.
*(Nhung trung tam nay thuong cung cap nhung dich vu giai tri mien phi quanh nam cho cac khach hang o moi do tuoi nhu nhac song va nhung man trinh dien dac biet.)*
</explanation>

<questions type="fill_blanks">
**Shopping centres**
1. attract a lot of customers during _______.
*(thu hut rat nhieu khach hang trong _______)*

2. allow customers to _______ on clothes and shoes.
*(cho phep khach hang _______ quan ao va giay dep)*

3. offer free _______ for customers of all ages.
*(cung cap _______ mien phi cho khach hang moi lua tuoi)*

4. are full of _______ during holidays.
*(tran day _______ trong ngay le)*

5. offer _______ air conditioning and heating.
*(cung cap suoi am va dieu hoa _______)*
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="60">

</teacher_script>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Ok dap an ne.

<answer>
**Dap an:** 1. sales | 2. try | 3. entertainment | 4. decorations | 5. free
</answer>

<explanation>
**Giai thich chi tiet:**

**1. sales** - attract a lot of customers during **sales**.
*(thu hut rat nhieu khach hang trong dot giam gia.)*
**Thong tin:** Shopping centres attract a lot of customers, especially at the weekend, on holidays, or during sales.

**2. try** - allow customers to **try** on clothes and shoes.
*(cho phep khach hang thu quan ao va giay dep.)*
**Thong tin:** Customers can touch the products and try on clothes and shoes.

**3. entertainment** - offer free **entertainment** for customers of all ages.
*(cung cap loai hinh giai tri mien phi cho cac khach hang moi lua tuoi.)*
**Thong tin:** These centres often offer year-round free entertainment for customers of all ages such as live music and special performances.

**4. decorations** - are full of **decorations** during holidays.
*(tran day nhung vat trang tri trong ngay le.)*
**Thong tin:** During holidays, shoppers can see decorations and join in the holiday excitement.

**5. free** - offer **free** air conditioning and heating.
*(cung cap suoi am va lam mat mien phi.)*
**Thong tin:** Shopping centres offer free air conditioning and heating.
</explanation>

</task>
