### Bài 2 trang 88 - Reading Comprehension

<teacher_script pause="120">
Bài 2 đọc đoạn văn và chọn đáp án đúng. 2 phút nha.
</teacher_script>

<reading>
**Why We Go to Shopping Centres**
Shopping centres attract a lot of customers... offering a wide range of products...
However, people also go for other reasons like entertainment (live music), hanging out with friends, or exercising in well-lit areas. Some go to avoid heat or cold because of free air conditioning.
</reading>

<questions type="multiple_choice">
**1.** People go to a shopping centre _______.
A. only for shopping | B. mainly to hang out | C. for many reasons

**2.** At shopping centres, customers can _______.
A. touch the products | B. bargain | C. pay to watch performances

**3.** A kind of entertainment there is _______.
A. fashion contests | B. live music | C. painting exhibitions

**4.** Some people exercise at a shopping centre because of its _______.
A. crowds | B. well-lit areas | C. shops

**5.** "Year-round" means _______.
A. always | B. sometimes | C. rarely
</questions>

<answer>
**Đáp án:** 1.C | 2.A | 3.B | 4.B | 5.A
</answer>

<explanation>
**Giải thích:**
1. **many reasons** (mua sắm, giải trí, đi dạo...).
2. **touch the products**.
3. **live music**.
4. **well-lit areas**.
5. **year-round** = always (quanh năm).
</explanation>
