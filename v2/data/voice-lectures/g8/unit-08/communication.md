<grammar>
## Making complaints (Dua ra loi phan nan)

**Than phien:**
- I'm calling to make a complaint about... (Toi goi de phan nan ve...)
- I'm not happy with... (Toi khong hai long ve...)

**Tra loi:**
- I'm sorry about that. (Toi xin loi ve dieu do.)
- Let me check it. (De toi kiem tra.)
- I'll send you another one / refund... (Toi se gui cai khac / hoan tien...)
</grammar>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Khi mua do bi loi hay khong vua y, minh co the phan nan lich su nha. Dung <eng>I'm calling to make a complaint about...</eng> hoac <eng>I'm not happy with...</eng>. Nguoi ban se xin loi va giai quyet cho minh.
</teacher_script>

<teacher_script pause="30">
Ghi vo tap: Phan nan = I'm calling to make a complaint about... / I'm not happy with... Tra loi = I'm sorry about that. / Let me check. 30 giay nha.
</teacher_script>

<reading>
**Tapescript - Bai nghe:**

**Mai:** I often go to the open-air market in my village. Most of the products there are home-grown or home-made. The market goers know one another and they chat happily while selling and buying.

**Nam:** When I need something, I go to a convenience store. They are easy to find because they are all over the place. The prices may be a bit higher than in supermarkets but they can save you time.

**Alice:** I love shopping at discount shops. There is a wide range of goods there, and everything is cheaper than at other places. Yesterday I bought a Christmas dress for half the price of one in a speciality shop.
</reading>

<translation>
**Dich bai nghe:**

**Mai:** Toi thuong di cho troi o lang toi. Hau het cac san pham o do deu do nha trong hoac tu lam. Nhung nguoi di cho quen biet nhau va ho tro chuyen vui ve trong khi mua ban.

**Nam:** Khi toi can thu gi do, toi den cua hang tien loi. Chung de tim vi co o khap moi noi. Gia co the cao hon mot chut so voi sieu thi nhung chung co the giup ban tiet kiem thoi gian.

**Alice:** Toi thich mua sam o cac cua hang giam gia. Co rat nhieu loai hang hoa o do va moi thu deu re hon nhung noi khac. Hom qua toi da mua mot chiec vay Giang sinh voi gia bang mot nua gia o mot cua hang chuyen dung.
</translation>

<explanation>
**Giai thich:**
- **Mai** thich cho troi (**open-air market**) o lang vi san pham nha trong (**home-grown**) va tu lam (**home-made**). Nguoi di cho quen biet nhau va tro chuyen vui ve.
- **Nam** thich cua hang tien loi (**convenience store**) vi de tim thay o khap noi va tiet kiem thoi gian (**save time**).
- **Alice** thich cua hang giam gia (**discount shop**) vi co nhieu loai hang hoa (**wide range of goods**) va moi thu deu re hon (**cheaper**).
</explanation>

### Bai 5 trang 86 - Group work

<teacher_script pause="0">
Bai 5 <eng>Work in groups. Share your favourite shopping place with your group.</eng> Lam viec nhom. Chia se dia diem mua sam yeu thich cua ban voi nhom nha.
</teacher_script>

<task>
**De:** Work in groups. Share your favourite shopping place with your group. You can include:
- the name of the place. *(ten cua dia diem)*
- the reason(s) why you like it. *(ly do vi sao ban thich no)*

**Dich de:** Lam viec nhom. Chia se dia diem mua sam yeu thich cua ban voi nhom. Ban co the bao gom:
- ten cua dia diem.
- ly do vi sao ban thich no.
</task>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Day la bai mau nha. Doc theo di.
</teacher_script>

<reading>
**Sample answer:**

I'm really into convenience store. It's simply convenient. You can find them everywhere you go. Convenience stores offer a smaller range of goods for you to buy. As there are not much goods, we don't waste time thinking about what to pick. Convenience store can contribute to a local business because they can sell what locals grow and make a lot of money.
</reading>

<translation>
**Dich bai mau:**

Minh rat thich cua hang tien loi. Don gian la vi no tien loi. Ban co the thay no o khap moi noi ban di. Cua hang tien loi cung cap it loai hang hoa hon de mua. Vi co it loai hang hoa hon nen chung ta se khong ton thoi gian suy nghi nen mua gi. Cua hang co the gop phan vao viec buon ban dia phuong vi ho co the ban nhung thu nguoi dan trong va kiem duoc nhieu tien.
</translation>

<teacher_script pause="0" action="record">
Bay gio toi luot em nha. Ghi am chia se ve dia diem mua sam yeu thich cua em di. Noi ten dia diem va ly do thich no.
</teacher_script>
