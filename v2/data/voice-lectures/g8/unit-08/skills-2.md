### Bài 2 trang 89 - Listening: Fill blanks

<teacher_script pause="60">
Bài 2 nghe về mua sắm trực tuyến và điền từ. 1 phút nha.
</teacher_script>

<audio src="https://img.loigiaihay.com/picture/2023/0803/track-51.mp3">
**Audio:** Online Shopping
</audio>

<questions type="fill_blanks">
**Word bank:** *seller's | money | shopaholic | shipping | over-shopping*

1. You can buy a product or service online... visit a _______ website.
2. Save time and _______.
3. If return, you pay for _______.
4. Become a _______.
5. Hard to stop _______ and buying unnecessarily.
</questions>

<answer>
**Đáp án:**
1. seller's
2. money
3. shipping
4. shopaholic
5. over-shopping
</answer>

<explanation>
**Giải thích:**
- Trang web người bán (seller's website).
- Tiết kiệm tiền (save money).
- Phí ship (shipping).
- Nghiện mua sắm (shopaholic).
- Mua quá mức (over-shopping).
</explanation>

### Bài 3 trang 89 - Listening: Multiple Choice

<teacher_script pause="60">
Bài 3 nghe lại và chọn đáp án đúng.
</teacher_script>

<audio src="https://img.loigiaihay.com/picture/2023/0803/track-52.mp3">
**Audio:** Listen again
</audio>

<questions type="multiple_choice">
**1.** When shopping online, you can pay in _______ ways.
A. one | B. two | C. three

**2.** The talk does NOT describe online shopping as _______.
A. convenient | B. easy | C. interesting

**3.** The talk is mainly about _______ of online shopping.
A. popularity | B. convenience | C. advantages and disadvantages
</questions>

<answer>
**Đáp án:** 1.B | 2.C | 3.C
</answer>

<explanation>
**Giải thích:**
1. **Two ways** (pay online or when get product).
2. **Interesting** (not mentioned).
3. **Advantages and disadvantages** (Convenient vs Shopaholic/Returns).
</explanation>

<teacher_script pause="0">

<reading>
**Sample: Benefits of Online Shopping**
There are many benefits of online shopping. First, it is very convenient. You can shop anytime and anywhere with just a phone or laptop. Second, it saves time because you don't have to travel to stores. Third, you can easily compare prices to find the best deal. Finally, there is a wider range of products online than in local shops.
</reading>
