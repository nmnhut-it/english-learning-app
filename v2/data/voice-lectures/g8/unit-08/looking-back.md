### Bài 1 trang 90 - Match meanings

<teacher_script pause="60">
Bài 1 nối từ với nghĩa của nó. 1 phút nha.
</teacher_script>

<task>
**Words:**
1. During sales
2. Browsing
3. Online shopping
4. Wide range of products
5. Shopaholic

**Meanings:**
a. Using Internet to order goods.
b. Time when prices are lower.
c. Person addicted to shopping.
d. Going round and looking.
e. Great number of goods.
</task>

<answer>
**Đáp án:** 1.b | 2.d | 3.a | 4.e | 5.c
</answer>

<explanation>
**Giải thích:**
- **During sales**: Time when prices are lower.
- **Browsing**: Going round and looking.
- **Online shopping**: Using Internet.
- **Wide range**: Great number.
- **Shopaholic**: Addicted person.
</explanation>

### Bài 3 trang 90 - Adverbs of frequency

<teacher_script pause="60">
Bài 3 điền trạng từ chỉ tần suất phù hợp.
</teacher_script>

<questions type="fill_blanks">
**1.** My sister _______ watches Master Chef. She never misses an episode.
**2.** My mum _______ shops at supermarket (but I don't go often).
**3.** My father _______ goes to dollar store. He says nothing worth buying.
**4.** We don't _______ go to supermarket. It's far.
**5.** These brands are _______ on sale, just once/twice a year.
</questions>

<answer>
**Đáp án:**
1. always
2. usually / often
3. never
4. often / usually
5. rarely
</answer>

### Bài 4 trang 90 - Correct tense

<teacher_script pause="60">
Bài 4 chia động từ (chú ý thì hiện tại đơn cho lịch trình tương lai).
</teacher_script>

<questions type="fill_blanks">
**1.** The shuttle bus (leave) _______ every 15 minutes.
**2.** What time (be) _______ the show tonight?
**3.** We (can / use) _______ these vouchers for next shopping.
**4.** The sale (last) _______ for just two hours tomorrow.
**5.** We (not / have) _______ home economics next semester.
</questions>

<answer>
**Đáp án:**
1. leaves
2. is
3. can use
4. lasts
5. don't have
</answer>

<teacher_script pause="30">
Sửa bài vào nha. Tất cả đều dùng thì hiện tại đơn (hoặc modal verb can) để chỉ lịch trình/khả năng trong tương lai.
</teacher_script>
