<reading>
**Tapescript - Skills 2**

**Reporter:** Hi! I'm talking to some students about their ideas for the 3Rs Club. First, what class are you in?

**Student 1:** I'm in class 6A.

**Reporter:** And what would you do if you became president of the 3Rs Club?

**Student 1:** I would put a recycling bin in every classroom. Students can put paper and plastic in different bins.

**Reporter:** Great! And what about you?

**Student 2:** I would organise a book fair at school. Students can exchange their used books for free. It would be really fun!

**Reporter:** What class are you in?

**Student 2:** Class 6E.

**Reporter:** Excellent! And you — what's your idea?

**Student 3:** My idea is to encourage students to go to school by bus instead of by motorbike. It helps reduce pollution.

**Reporter:** Wonderful! And the last student?

**Student 4:** I would organise a uniform fair so students can exchange their old uniforms. Some uniforms are still in good condition!

**Reporter:** These are all fantastic ideas! Thank you, everyone.
</reading>

<task>
**Đề:** Listen again and decide if the statements are True (T) or False (F).

<questions type="true_false">
**1.** Student 1 is in class 6B.
*Học sinh 1 ở lớp 6B.*

**2.** Student 2 wants to organise a book fair at school.
*Học sinh 2 muốn tổ chức hội chợ sách ở trường.*

**3.** Student 3 wants students to cycle to school.
*Học sinh 3 muốn học sinh đạp xe đến trường.*

**4.** Student 4's idea is about exchanging old uniforms.
*Ý tưởng của học sinh 4 là về việc trao đổi đồng phục cũ.*
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Đáp án nè.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Đáp án:** 1.F | 2.T | 3.F | 4.T
</answer>

<explanation>
1. **F** — Student 1 là lớp **6A**, không phải 6B.
2. **T** — *"organise a book fair at school"* — đúng.
3. **F** — Student 3 muốn đi học bằng **bus** (xe buýt), không phải đạp xe (*cycle*).
4. **T** — *"organise a uniform fair so students can exchange their old uniforms"* — đúng.
</explanation>

<answer>

My classmate is Thanh. If she becomes the president of the 3Rs Club, she will do two things. Firstly, she will organise weekly fairs for students to exchange their old things. </answer>
