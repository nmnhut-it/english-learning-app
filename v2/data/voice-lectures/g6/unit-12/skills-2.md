### Tapescript

<reading>
**Khang:** Dr Adams, please tell me something about robots.

**Dr Adams:** Sure. Robots can do quite a lot today. Home robots can do housework. Doctor robots can look after sick people. And...

**Khang:** Can robots build houses?

**Dr Adams:** Yes. Worker robots can even build very high buildings.

**Khang:** Can they teach?

**Dr Adams:** Yes. Teacher robots can teach many subjects in a classroom or online.

**Khang:** Can they understand what we say?

**Dr Adams:** Yes, they do. They can even speak to us.

**Khang:** Robots can do everything like humans?

**Dr Adams:** No, not everything. They can't understand our feelings or play football.
</reading>

<translation>
**Khang:** Tien si Adams, vui long cho toi biet vai dieu ve robot.

**Tien si Adams:** Chac chan. Ngay nay robot co the lam duoc kha nhieu viec. Robot nha co the lam viec nha. Robot bac si co the cham soc nguoi benh. Va...

**Khang:** Robot co the xay nha khong?

**Tien si Adams:** Vang. Robot cong nhan tham chi co the xay dung cac toa nha rat cao.

**Khang:** Ho co the day khong?

**Tien si Adams:** Vang. Robot giao vien co the day nhieu mon hoc trong mot lop hoc hoac truc tuyen.

**Khang:** Ho co the hieu nhung gi chung ta noi khong?

**Tien si Adams:** Dung vay. Ho tham chi co the noi chuyen voi chung ta.

**Khang:** Robot co the lam moi thu nhu con nguoi?

**Tien si Adams:** Khong, khong phai tat ca. Ho khong the hieu cam giac cua chung ta hoac choi bong.
</translation>

<questions type="true_false">
**1.** Robots can't do many things today.
*Ngay nay robot khong the lam duoc nhieu viec.*

**2.** Worker robots can build very high buildings.
*Robot cong nhan co the xay dung cac toa nha rat cao.*

**3.** Teacher robots can teach on the internet.
*Robot giao vien co the day tren internet.*

**4.** Robots can talk to humans.
*Robot co the noi chuyen voi con nguoi.*

**5.** Robots can do everything like humans.
*Robot co the lam moi thu nhu con nguoi.*
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="60">

</teacher_script>

<teacher_script pause="0">

<answer>
**Dap an:** 1.F | 2.T | 3.T | 4.T | 5.F
</answer>

<explanation>
**1. F** - Robots can't do many things today. => SAI. Dr Adams noi "Robots can do quite a lot today" - robot CO THE lam kha nhieu viec.

**2. T** - Worker robots can build very high buildings. => DUNG. Dr Adams noi "Worker robots can even build very high buildings."

**3. T** - Teacher robots can teach on the internet. => DUNG. Dr Adams noi "Teacher robots can teach many subjects in a classroom or online."

**4. T** - Robots can talk to humans. => DUNG. Dr Adams noi "They can even speak to us."

**5. F** - Robots can do everything like humans. => SAI. Dr Adams noi "No, not everything. They can't understand our feelings or play football."
</explanation>

### Bai 4 trang 65 - Write a paragraph

<teacher_script pause="0">
Bai 4, <eng>Now write a paragraph of 50-60 words about the robot you would like to have. Use the information in 3 to help you.</eng> Bay gio, hay viet mot doan van gom 50-60 tu ve robot ma ban muon co. Su dung thong tin o bai 3 de giup ban. 5 phut nha.
</teacher_script>

<task>
**De:** Now write a paragraph of 50-60 words about the robot you would like to have. Use the information in 3 to help you.
**Dich de:** Bay gio, hay viet mot doan van gom 50-60 tu ve cac robot ma ban muon co. Su dung thong tin bai 3 de giup ban.
</task>

<teacher_script pause="300" action="photo">
Viet doan van ve robot cua cac em. 5 phut. Xong roi chup anh gui len nha.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Mau tra loi:**

My robot's name is Funny. It is an entertainment robot. It can work at home and at school. It can sing, dance and tell stories to make me happy. It can also help me to do my homework and practice English. I like it so much.

*(Ten robot cua toi la Funny. No la mot robot giai tri. No co the hoat dong o nha va o truong. No co the hat, nhay va ke nhung cau chuyen de lam toi vui. No cung co the giup toi lam bai tap ve nha va thuc hanh tieng Anh. Toi thich no rat nhieu.)*
</answer>

<explanation>
**Cach viet:**
1. Gioi thieu ten va loai robot: My robot's name is... It is a... robot.
2. Noi no co the lam viec o dau: It can work at...
3. Noi no co the lam gi: It can...
4. Ket luan: I like/love it.
</explanation>
