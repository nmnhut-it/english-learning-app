### Grammar: Superlative adjectives (So sanh nhat)

<teacher_script pause="0">
Truoc tien, chung ta on lai ve tinh tu so sanh nhat nha. Chung ta su dung tinh tu so sanh nhat de so sanh ba hoac nhieu nguoi hoac nhieu su vat voi nhau.
</teacher_script>

<grammar>
**Superlative adjectives - Tinh tu so sanh nhat (Tinh tu ngan)**

Chung ta su dung tinh tu so sanh nhat de so sanh ba hoac nhieu nguoi/su vat.

**Cong thuc:**
- So sanh nhat: **the + tinh tu + est**

**Quy tac:**
| Loai tinh tu | Vi du | So sanh nhat | Quy tac |
|--------------|-------|--------------|---------|
| 1 am tiet | fast | fastest | + est |
| | tall | tallest | + est |
| Ket thuc bang -e | nice | nicest | + st |
| | large | largest | + st |
| Nguyen am + phu am | big | biggest | Gap doi phu am + est |
| | hot | hottest | Gap doi phu am + est |
| Ket thuc bang -y | noisy | noisiest | Doi y thanh i + est |
| | heavy | heaviest | Doi y thanh i + est |
</grammar>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Nho nha: Tinh tu ngan 1 am tiet thi them -est. Tinh tu ket thuc bang -e thi chi them -st. Tinh tu co 1 nguyen am + 1 phu am o cuoi thi gap doi phu am roi them -est. Tinh tu ket thuc bang -y thi doi y thanh i roi them -est.
</teacher_script>

<questions type="fill_blanks">

|---------------------|-------------------------------------|
| fast (nhanh) | ________ |

| noisy (on ao) | ________ |
| nice (tuyet voi) | ________ |

<teacher_script pause="60">

<teacher_script pause="0">
Ok dap an ne.

<answer>
**Dap an:**
- fast -> fastest
- tall -> tallest
- noisy -> noisiest
- nice -> nicest
- hot -> hottest
- light -> lightest
- quiet -> quietest
- heavy -> heaviest
- large -> largest
</answer>

<explanation>
- **fast -> fastest**: Tinh tu 1 am tiet, them -est.
- **tall -> tallest**: Tinh tu 1 am tiet, them -est.
- **noisy -> noisiest**: Ket thuc bang -y, doi y thanh i + est.
- **nice -> nicest**: Ket thuc bang -e, chi them -st.
- **hot -> hottest**: 1 nguyen am (o) + 1 phu am (t), gap doi phu am + est.
- **light -> lightest**: Tinh tu 1 am tiet, them -est.
- **quiet -> quietest**: Tinh tu 2 am tiet nhung ket thuc bang -et, them -est.
- **heavy -> heaviest**: Ket thuc bang -y, doi y thanh i + est.
- **large -> largest**: Ket thuc bang -e, chi them -st.
</explanation>

### Bai 2 trang 61 - Complete sentences

<teacher_script pause="0">
Bai 2, <eng>Complete the following sentences with the superlative form of the adjectives in brackets. 1 is an example.</eng> Hoan thanh cac cau sau voi dang so sanh nhat cua cac tinh tu trong ngoac. Cau 1 la vi du. 1 phut nha.
</teacher_script>

<task>
**De:** Complete the following sentences with the superlative form of the adjectives in brackets. 1 is an example.
**Dich de:** Hoan thanh cac cau sau voi dang so sanh nhat cua cac tinh tu trong ngoac. Cau 1 la vi du minh hoa.
</task>

<questions type="fill_blanks">
**1.** Bonbon can move 10 tons; it's the **strongest** of all. (strong)
*Bonbon co the di chuyen 10 tan; no manh nhat trong tat ca.*

**2.** This robot can understand all of what we say. It's the ________ in the robot show. (smart)
*Robot nay co the hieu tat ca nhung gi chung ta noi. No ________ trong trien lam robot.*

**3.** He is the ________ in our class; he is about 1.8 m tall. (tall)
*Anh ay ________ trong lop cua chung toi; anh ay cao khoang 1,8m.*

**4.** This is the ________ of all home robots; we can put it in our bag. (small)
*Day la robot ________ trong so tat ca cac robot gia dinh; chung toi co the dat no trong tui.*

**5.** This robot is only 200 dollars. It's the ________ in our shop. (cheap)
*Robot nay chi co 200 do la. No ________ trong cua hang cua chung toi.*
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="60">
Lam bai di. 1 phut.
</teacher_script>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Ok dap an ne.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Dap an:** 2.smartest | 3.tallest | 4.smallest | 5.cheapest
</answer>

<explanation>
**2. smartest** - smart + est = smartest (thong minh nhat). Robot hieu duoc tat ca => thong minh nhat.

**3. tallest** - tall + est = tallest (cao nhat). Cao 1.8m trong lop => cao nhat.

**4. smallest** - small + est = smallest (nho nhat). Co the bo vao tui => nho nhat.

**5. cheapest** - cheap + est = cheapest (re nhat). Chi 200 do la => re nhat.
</explanation>

### Bai 3 trang 62 - Comparative or Superlative

<teacher_script pause="0">
Bai 3, <eng>Complete the following sentences with comparative or superlative form of the adjectives in brackets.</eng> Hoan thanh cac cau sau voi dang so sanh hon hoac so sanh nhat cua cac tinh tu trong ngoac. Chu y phan biet so sanh hon dung -er, so sanh nhat dung the...-est nha. 1 phut.
</teacher_script>

<task>
**De:** Complete the following sentences with comparative or superlative form of the adjectives in brackets.
**Dich de:** Hoan thanh cac cau sau voi dang so sanh hon hoac so sanh nhat cua cac tinh tu trong ngoac.
</task>

<questions type="fill_blanks">
**1.** My brother has a ________ room than me. (tidy)
*Anh trai toi co mot can phong ________ toi.*

**2.** The ________ desert of all is Sahara and it's in Africa. (hot)
*Sa mac ________ la Sahara va no o Chau Phi.*

**3.** Travelling by plane is ________ than going by car. (fast)
*Di may bay ________ di o to.*

**4.** Who is the ________ in your family? (tall)
*Ai ________ trong gia dinh ban?*

**5.** I think dogs are ________ than cats. (smart)
*Toi nghi cho ________ meo.*
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="60">
Lam bai di. 1 phut.
</teacher_script>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Ok dap an ne.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Dap an:** 1.tidier | 2.hottest | 3.faster | 4.tallest | 5.smarter
</answer>

<explanation>
**1. tidier** - So sanh hon vi co "than". Tidy ket thuc bang -y nen doi y thanh i + er = tidier.

**2. hottest** - So sanh nhat vi co "of all". Hot co 1 nguyen am + 1 phu am nen gap doi phu am + est = hottest.

**3. faster** - So sanh hon vi co "than". Fast + er = faster.

**4. tallest** - So sanh nhat vi co "the" va hoi trong gia dinh (nhieu nguoi). Tall + est = tallest.

**5. smarter** - So sanh hon vi co "than". Smart + er = smarter.
</explanation>

| | M10 | H9 | A3 |

**Example:**
A: A3 is the tallest of the three robots. (A3 cao nhat trong ba robot.)
B: M10 is the youngest of the three robots. (M10 la robot nho tuoi nhat trong ba robot.)

### Bai 5 trang 62 - Game: Find someone who...

<teacher_script pause="0">

<task>
**De:** GAME: Find someone who... Work in pairs. Ask your partner to find in your class someone who is...
**Dich de:** Tro choi: Tim ra nguoi nao do... Lam viec theo cap. Yeu cau ban cua em tim trong lop cua ban ai do la...

- the oldest (lon tuoi nhat)

</task>

<answer>
