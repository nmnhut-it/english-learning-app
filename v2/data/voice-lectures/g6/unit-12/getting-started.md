<task>
**De:** Read the conversation and tick (T) True or (F) False.
**Dich de:** Doc bai hoi thoai va danh dau T (Dung) hoac F (Sai).

<questions type="true_false">
**1.** H8 is a very useful robot.
*H8 la mot robot rat huu ich.*

**2.** WB2 can't repair broken machines.
*WB2 khong the sua chua may bi hong.*

**3.** Shifa is a doctor robot.
*Shifa la mot robot bac si.*

**4.** H8 is the fastest in the robot show.
*H8 nhanh nhat trong trien lam robot.*

**5.** Shifa is very smart.
*Shifa rat thong minh.*
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="60">
Lam bai di. 1 phut.
</teacher_script>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Ok dap an ne.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Dap an:** 1.T | 2.F | 3.T | 4.F | 5.T
</answer>

<explanation>
**1. T** - H8 is a very useful robot. => DUNG. Trong hoi thoai Nick noi "It looks very useful!" khi noi ve H8.

**2. F** - WB2 can't repair broken machines. => SAI. Dr Adams noi WB2 "can move heavy things or repair broken machines" - WB2 CO THE sua may hong.

**3. T** - Shifa is a doctor robot. => DUNG. Dr Adams noi "It's Shifa, a doctor robot."

**4. F** - H8 is the fastest in the robot show. => SAI. WB2 moi la robot nhanh nhat: "it's the strongest and fastest robot here."

**5. T** - Shifa is very smart. => DUNG. Dr Adams noi "It's the smartest robot."
</explanation>

### Bai 3 trang 59 - Fill blanks

<teacher_script pause="0">
Bai 3, <eng>Complete the following sentences, using the adjectives in the box</eng>. Hoan thanh cac cau sau, su dung cac tinh tu trong hop. 1 phut nha.

<task>
**De:** Complete the following sentences, using the adjectives in the box.
**Dich de:** Hoan thanh cac cau sau, su dung cac tinh tu trong hop.

| fast | smart | useful | heavy | strong |
</task>

<questions type="fill_blanks">
**1.** My dad bought me a very ________ home robot last week. It helps me to do many household chores.
*Bo toi da mua cho toi mot con ro bot rat ________ vao tuan truoc. No giup toi lam nhieu viec nha.*

**2.** This is a very ________ car. It can travel at a speed of 300 km per hour.
*Day la mot chiec xe rat ________. No co the di chuyen voi toc do 300 km mot gio.*

**3.** He's very ________. He can move a big car!
*Anh ay rat ________. Anh ay co the di chuyen mot chiec o to to!*

**4.** They're making a very ________ robot. It can understand 30 languages.
*Ho dang lam mot nguoi may rat ________. No co the hieu 30 ngon ngu.*

**5.** The table is too ________ for me to move on my own.
*Ban qua ________ de toi co the tu di chuyen.*
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="60">
Lam bai di. 1 phut.
</teacher_script>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Ok dap an ne.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Dap an:** 1.useful | 2.fast | 3.strong | 4.smart | 5.heavy
</answer>

<explanation>
**1. useful** - Robot giup lam viec nha => huu ich (useful).

**2. fast** - Xe chay 300 km/h => nhanh (fast).

**3. strong** - Di chuyen duoc o to to => khoe/manh (strong).

**4. smart** - Hieu 30 ngon ngu => thong minh (smart).

**5. heavy** - Khong the tu di chuyen duoc => nang (heavy).
</explanation>
