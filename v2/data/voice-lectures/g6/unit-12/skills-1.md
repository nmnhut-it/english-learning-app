### Reading - Robot Show in Ha Noi

<teacher_script pause="0">
Bay gio cac em doc bai doc nha. Doc ky de tra loi cau hoi.
</teacher_script>

<reading>
Today there is an international robot show in Ha Noi. People can see many types of robots there.

Home robots are useful for housework. They can do most of the housework: cook meals, clean the house, do the washing, and iron clothes.

Teacher robots are the best choice for children. They can help them to study. They can teach them English, literature, maths and other subjects. They can also help children to improve their English pronunciation.

People are also interested in other types of robots at the show. Worker robots can build houses and move heavy things. Doctor robots can look after sick people and space robots can build space stations on the Moon and on planets.
</reading>

<translation>
Hom nay co mot trien lam robot quoc te tai Ha Noi. Moi nguoi co the thay nhieu loai robot o do.

Robot gia dinh rat huu ich cho cong viec noi tro. Ho co the lam hau het cac cong viec nha: nau an, don dep nha cua, giat giu, ui quan ao.

Robot giao vien la su lua chon tot nhat cho tre em. Ho co the giup ho hoc tap. Ho co the day chung tieng Anh, van hoc, toan hoc va cac mon hoc khac. Ho cung co the giup tre em cai thien kha nang phat am tieng Anh cua minh.

Moi nguoi cung quan tam den cac loai robot khac tai trien lam. Robot cong nhan co the xay nha va di chuyen nhung thu nang nhoc. Robot bac si co the cham soc nguoi benh va robot vu tru co the xay dung cac tram vu tru tren Mat trang va tren cac hanh tinh.
</translation>

### Bai 2 trang 64 - Multiple choice

<teacher_script pause="0">
Bai 2, <eng>Read the text and choose the best answer to each of the questions.</eng> Doc van ban va chon cau tra loi dung nhat cho moi cau hoi. 1 phut nha.
</teacher_script>

<task>
**De:** Read the text and choose the best answer to each of the questions.
**Dich de:** Doc van ban va chon cau tra loi dung nhat cho moi cau hoi.
</task>

<questions type="multiple_choice">
**1.** What show is on in Ha Noi now?
*Hien tai Ha Noi dang co chuong trinh gi?*
- A. A fashion show *(Mot buoi bieu dien thoi trang)*
- B. A robot show *(Mot buoi trien lam robot)*
- C. A pet show *(Mot buoi bieu dien thu cung)*

**2.** Which type of robots can help children in their study?
*Loai robot nao co the giup tre trong hoc tap?*
- A. Home robots *(Robot nha)*
- B. Worker robots *(Nguoi may cong nhan)*
- C. Teacher robots *(Nguoi may giao vien)*

**3.** Doctor robots can ________.
*Robot bac si co the ________.*
- A. help children to study *(giup tre em hoc tap)*
- B. build houses *(xay nha)*
- C. take care of sick people *(cham soc nguoi om)*

**4.** Which of the following sentences is NOT true according to the passage?
*Cau nao sau day KHONG dung theo doan van?*
- A. Worker robots can build houses and move heavy things. *(Robot cong nhan co the xay nha va di chuyen nhung thu nang nhoc.)*
- B. Space robots can build space stations on the Moon. *(Nguoi may khong gian co the xay dung cac Tram vu tru tren Mat trang.)*
- C. Home robots can't do much of the housework. *(Robot gia dinh khong the lam nhieu viec nha.)*
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="60">
Lam bai di. 1 phut.
</teacher_script>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Ok dap an ne.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Dap an:** 1.B | 2.C | 3.C | 4.C
</answer>

<explanation>
**1. B** - A robot show. Bai doc noi "there is an international robot show in Ha Noi".

**2. C** - Teacher robots. Bai doc noi "Teacher robots are the best choice for children. They can help them to study."

**3. C** - take care of sick people. Bai doc noi "Doctor robots can look after sick people".

**4. C** - Home robots can't do much of the housework. => SAI. Bai doc noi "They can do most of the housework" - robot gia dinh CO THE lam hau het viec nha.
</explanation>
