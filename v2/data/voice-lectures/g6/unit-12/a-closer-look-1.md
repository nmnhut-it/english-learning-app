### Pronunciation - Falling tone in statements

<teacher_script pause="0">
Phan <eng>Pronunciation</eng>. Hom nay chung ta hoc ve ngu dieu xuong giong trong cau tran thuat nha. Trong cau ke, chung ta xuong giong o cuoi cau.
</teacher_script>

<grammar>
**Falling tone in statements (Xuong giong trong cau tran thuat)**

Trong tieng Anh, khi noi cau tran thuat (cau ke), chung ta thuong xuong giong o cuoi cau.

**Vi du:**
- I often water plants after school. ↘
- Shifa can do many things like humans. ↘
</grammar>
