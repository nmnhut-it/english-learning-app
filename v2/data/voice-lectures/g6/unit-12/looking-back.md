### Bai 3 trang 66 - Complete paragraph with superlative

<teacher_script pause="0">
Bai 3, phan <eng>Grammar</eng>. <eng>Use the correct form of the adjectives in brackets to complete the paragraph.</eng> Su dung dang dung cua cac tinh tu trong ngoac de hoan thanh doan van. Nho so sanh nhat la the + tinh tu + est nha. 1 phut.
</teacher_script>

<task>
**De:** Use the correct form of the adjectives in brackets to complete the paragraph.
**Dich de:** Su dung dang dung cua cac tinh tu trong ngoac de hoan thanh doan van.
</task>

<questions type="fill_blanks">
Welcome to our company. We've made the (1. smart) ________ robots in the world. It's the (2. small) ________ and the (3. light) ________ we've made but it can do many things in your house. It can even fly to clean the ceilings. It can teach children all school subjects. It's also the (4. strong) ________ in our lab because it can move more than 200 kg. It's not difficult to own a robot like this because it's one of the (5. cheap) ________ robots in the world.
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="60">
Lam bai di. 1 phut.
</teacher_script>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Ok dap an ne.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Dap an:** 1.smartest | 2.smallest | 3.lightest | 4.strongest | 5.cheapest
</answer>

<explanation>
Tat ca deu la so sanh nhat vi dang so sanh voi "in the world" (tren the gioi) hoac "in our lab" (trong phong thi nghiem).

**1. smartest** - smart + est = smartest (thong minh nhat)
**2. smallest** - small + est = smallest (nho nhat)
**3. lightest** - light + est = lightest (nhe nhat)
**4. strongest** - strong + est = strongest (manh nhat)
**5. cheapest** - cheap + est = cheapest (re nhat)
</explanation>

<translation>
Chao mung den voi cong ty cua chung toi. Chung toi da tao ra robot thong minh nhat tren the gioi. No nho nhat va nhe nhat ma chung toi da che tao nhung no co the lam duoc nhieu thu trong ngoi nha cua ban tham chi co the bay de lam sach tran nha. No co the day tre con tat ca cac mon hoc o truong. No cung la loai manh nhat trong phong thi nghiem cua chung toi vi no co the di chuyen hon 200 kg. Khong kho de so huu mot con robot nhu the nay vi no la mot trong nhung con robot re nhat the gioi.
</translation>

<questions type="fill_blanks">
**1.** What is the (high) ________ mountain in the world?
*Ngon nui nao ________ the gioi?*

**2.** What is the (large) ________ lake in Viet Nam?
*Ho nao ________ Viet Nam?*

**3.** What is the (long) ________ beach in Viet Nam?
*Bai bien nao ________ Viet Nam?*

**4.** What is the (wide) ________ river in the world?
*Con song nao ________ tren the gioi?*

**5.** What is the (hot) ________ desert in the world?
*Sa mac nao ________ tren the gioi?*
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="60">
Lam bai di. 1 phut.
</teacher_script>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Ok dap an ne.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Dap an:** 1.highest | 2.largest | 3.longest | 4.widest | 5.hottest
</answer>

<explanation>
**1. highest** - high + est = highest (cao nhat). Ngon nui cao nhat the gioi la Everest.

**2. largest** - large + st = largest (lon nhat). Ho lon nhat Viet Nam co the la Ba Be hoac Dau Tieng.

**3. longest** - long + est = longest (dai nhat). Bai bien dai nhat Viet Nam la bai bien Tra Co.

**4. widest** - wide + st = widest (rong nhat). Con song rong nhat the gioi la Amazon.

**5. hottest** - hot + test = hottest (nong nhat). Gap doi phu am "t" vi truoc no la 1 nguyen am "o". Sa mac nong nhat la Sahara.
</explanation>
