### Bai 2 trang 63 - Express opinions

<teacher_script pause="0">
Bai 2, <eng>Work in pairs. Express your opinions about the following statements. Use the highlighted phrases in the dialogues in 1 as cues.</eng> Lam viec theo cap. Hay bay to y kien cua ban ve nhung cau sau. Su dung cac cum tu duoc danh dau trong cac doan hoi thoai o bai 1.
</teacher_script>

<task>
**De:** Work in pairs. Express your opinions about the following statements. Use the highlighted phrases in the dialogues in 1 as cues.
**Dich de:** Lam viec theo cap. Hay bay to y kien cua ban ve nhung cau sau. Su dung cac cum tu duoc danh dau trong cac doan hoi thoai o bai 1 nhu nhung goi y.
</task>

<questions type="speaking">
**1.** Home robots are the most useful of all types of robots.
*Robot gia dinh la loai robot huu ich nhat trong so cac loai robot.*

**2.** Some people can use robots to do bad things.
*Mot so nguoi co the su dung robot de lam nhung dieu xau.*

**3.** Robots will use too much electricity in the future.
*Robot se su dung qua nhieu dien trong tuong lai.*
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="0" action="record">
Cac em bay to y kien cua minh nha. Vi du: <eng>Home robots are the most useful of all types of robots. I agree with you.</eng> hoac <eng>I don't agree with you.</eng> Bam nut ghi am.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Mau tra loi:**

**1.**
A: Home robots are the most useful of all types of robots. (Robot gia dinh la loai robot huu ich nhat.)
B: I agree with you. (Toi dong y voi ban.)

**2.**
A: Some people can use robots to do bad things. (Mot so nguoi co the su dung robot de lam nhung dieu xau.)
B: I agree with you. / I don't agree with you. (Toi dong y voi ban. / Toi khong dong y voi ban.)

**3.**
A: Robots will use too much electricity in the future. (Robot se su dung qua nhieu dien trong tuong lai.)
B: I agree with you. / I don't agree with you. (Toi dong y voi ban. / Toi khong dong y voi ban.)
</answer>

### Bai 3 trang 63 - Listen and fill blanks

<teacher_script pause="0">
Bai 3, <eng>Listen to the radio programme from 4Teen News. Then fill the blanks with the words you hear.</eng> Nghe chuong trinh radio tu 4Teen News. Sau do dien nhung tu ban nghe duoc vao cho trong. 1 phut nha.
</teacher_script>

<task>
**De:** Listen to the radio programme from 4Teen News. Then fill the blanks with the words you hear.
**Dich de:** Nghe chuong trinh radio tu 4Teen News. Sau do dien nhung tu ban nghe duoc vao cho trong.
</task>

<questions type="fill_blanks">
**Speaker:** Today we asked our friends: Tom from Australia, Linh from Viet Nam and Nobita from Japan to tell us about their robots. Tom, would you like to start?
*Dien gia: Hom nay chung ta nho cac ban cua minh: Tom den tu Uc, Linh den tu Viet Nam va Nobita den tu Nhat Ban ke cho chung ta nghe ve robot cua ho. Tom, ban co muon bat dau khong?*

**Tom:** Well, my robot can (1) ________ what I say. It can also understand my feelings. It's the (2) ________ robot.
*Tom: O, robot cua toi co the (1) ________ nhung gi toi noi. No cung co the hieu duoc cam xuc cua toi. Do la robot (2) ________.*

**Speaker:** Linh?
*Dien gia: Linh?*

**Linh:** My robot is my best friend. It does a lot for me: clean the floor, (3) ________ my toys away, and...
*Linh: Robot la ban than nhat cua toi. No lam duoc rat nhieu dieu doi voi toi: lau san nha, (3) ________ do choi cua toi di, va...*

**Speaker:** And Nobita?
*Dien gia: Con Nobita?*

**Nobita:** My robot is very useful. It helps me a lot. It can (4) ________ my plants and even work as a (5) ________.
*Nobita: Robot cua toi rat huu ich. No giup toi rat nhieu. No co the (4) ________ cay cua toi va tham chi hoat dong nhu mot nguoi (5) ________.*
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="60">
Nghe va dien vao cho trong. 1 phut.
</teacher_script>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Ok dap an ne.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Dap an:** 1.understand | 2.smartest | 3.put | 4.water | 5.guard
</answer>

<explanation>
**1. understand** - Tom noi robot co the hieu nhung gi anh ay noi.
**2. smartest** - Robot thong minh nhat vi hieu duoc cam xuc.
**3. put** - Linh noi robot cat do choi di (put my toys away).
**4. water** - Nobita noi robot tuoi cay (water my plants).
**5. guard** - Nobita noi robot lam bao ve (work as a guard).
</explanation>
