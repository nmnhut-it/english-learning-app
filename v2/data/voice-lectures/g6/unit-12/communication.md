**UNIT 12: ROBOTS**

**COMMUNICATION**   
## Bài 1 (Listening and reading dialogues)

This is a listening exercise with two dialogues about expressing agreement and disagreement.

## Bài 2 (Express opinions)

A. \- Home robots are the most useful of all types of robots.

- I don't agree with you

B. \- Some people can use robots to do bad things.

- I agree with you

C. \- Robots will use too much electricity in the future.

- I don't agree with you

## Bài 3 (Listen and fill in the blanks)

1. understand  
2. smartest  
3. put  
4. water  
5. guard

## Bài 4 (Interview about robot abilities)

Sample answers provided in the table:

- Nam: Clean house and cook meals  
- Linh: Sing songs with her, draw pictures  
- Mai: Do homework and play badminton
