**Writing**   
**Chủ từ \+ should \+ V0: Ai đó nên làm gì**  
**Chủ từ \+ shouldn’t \+ V0: Ai đó không nên làm gì**   
**Bài 3 trang 65** 

1. Children should clean and decorate our home.   
2. Children should wish for good luck.   
3. Children shouldn’t ask for lucky money.   
4. Children shouldn’t break things. 

**Bài 4 trang 65**   
Dear Tom,  
Tet is coming. I will tell you more about our Tet.   
At Tet, we should **visit relatives with parents**. We should **wear beautiful clothes** too. But we shouldn't **break things**. We shouldn't **play cards all night**, either.  
Please write and tell me about your New Year celebration.  
Yours,

Nguyen
