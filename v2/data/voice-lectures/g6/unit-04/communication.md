**UNIT 4: MY NEIGHBOURHOOD**

**COMMUNICATION**   
**Hỏi đường và chỉ đường**   
Could/Can you show/tell me the way to \+ địa điểm, please?   
\= Where is the nearest \+ địa điểm, please?   
\= Bạn có thể chỉ cho cho tôi đường tới …. /Cái … gần nhất là ở đâu?   
**Bài 3 trang 43** 

1. straight   
2. second  
3. right   
4. next to 

**Bài 4 trang 43**: Viết một đoạn hướng dẫn đi vòng quận 1 \- TPHCM theo bản đồ  
Welcome to Ho Chi Minh city. As you know, It is not only the largest city in south Vietnam but also is a historic city where many important events of the country took place. And it has many beautiful places and restaurants. OK\! Let’s start our tour \! First , go to Saigon Opera House . To get there, walk about for 5 minutes. The opera house is on your right. Next, go straight and turn right at Dong Khoi street and go to the Duc Ba Cathedral.
