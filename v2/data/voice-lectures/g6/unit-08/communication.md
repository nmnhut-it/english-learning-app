<grammar>
## Cau truc giao tiep: Chuc mung

### 1. Chuc mung ai do
| English | Vietnamese |
|---------|------------|
| **Congratulations!** | Chuc mung ban! |
| **Congratulations on your win!** | Chuc mung chien thang cua ban! |
| **Well done!** | Lam tot lam! |

### 2. Dap lai loi chuc mung
| English | Vietnamese |
|---------|------------|
| **Thank you!** | Cam on! |
| **Thanks!** | Cam on! |
| **It's very kind of you.** | Ban that tot. |
</grammar>

<teacher_script pause="0">
<eng>Congratulations!</eng> - Chuc mung! Khi ban cua minh thang tran, do dai hoc, hay dat duoc thanh tich gi do thi noi <eng>Congratulations!</eng> nha.
</teacher_script>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Nguoi duoc chuc mung dap lai sao? Don gian la <eng>Thank you!</eng> - Cam on! thoi. Hoac lich su hon thi noi <eng>It's very kind of you</eng> - Ban that tot.
</teacher_script>

<teacher_script pause="30">
Ghi vo tap: Chuc mung = <eng>Congratulations!</eng>, Dap lai = <eng>Thank you!</eng> 30 giay nha.
</teacher_script>

### Bai 2 trang 21 - Work in pairs

<teacher_script pause="0">

</teacher_script>

<task>
**De:** Work in pairs. Student A has won a prize in the school's singing contest / sports competition. Student B congratulates him / her. Make a similar dialogue. Remember to use the highlighted sentences in 1. Then change roles.
**Dich de:** Lam viec theo cap. Hoc sinh A da chien thang mot cuoc thi hat/ tranh tai the thao cua truong. Hoc sinh B chuc mung ban ay. Tao doan hoi thoai tuong tu. Nho su dung cac cau duoc lam noi bat o bai 1. Sau do doi vai.
</task>

<dialogue>
**Hoi thoai mau:**

| English | Vietnamese |
|---------|------------|
| **My:** Last week I won the first prize of my school's singing contest. | **My:** Tuan truoc, minh da gianh duoc giai nhat trong cuoc thi hat cua truong. |
| **Minh:** Congratulations! | **Minh:** Chuc mung ban nhe! |
| **My:** Thank you, Minh. | **My:** Cam on Minh. |
| **Minh:** So you're your school's idol. Anyway, last week I also won the championship of my school's badminton competition. | **Minh:** Vay ban la than tuong cua truong roi. A nay, tuan truoc minh cung da gianh chuc vo dich trong cuoc thi cau long cua truong day. |
| **My:** Congratulations! | **My:** Chuc mung ban nha! |
| **Minh:** It's very kind of you. Thanks, My. | **Minh:** Ban rat tot. Cam on, My. |
</dialogue>

### Bai 3 trang 21 - Sports Quiz

<teacher_script pause="0">
Bai 3 <eng>The sports/ game you like</eng> - Mon the thao/ tro choi em thich nha.
</teacher_script>

<task>
**De:** Work in pairs. Do the quiz.
**Dich de:** Lam viec theo cap. Giai cau do.
</task>

<teacher_script pause="90">
Day la cau do ve the thao. Lam theo cap roi tra loi cau hoi di. 1 phut 30 giay. Xem minh biet bao nhieu ve the thao nha.
</teacher_script>

<questions type="fill_blanks">
**1.** How many players are there in a football match?

*Co bao nhieu nguoi choi trong mot tran dau bong da?*

**2.** How long does a football match last?

*Mot tran bong da keo dai bao lau?*

**3.** How often do the Olympic Games take place?

*The van hoi Olympic thuong dien ra bao lau mot lan?*

**4.** How long is a marathon?

*Mot cuoc chay marathon dai bao nhieu?*

**5.** Where did the first Olympic Games take place?

*The van hoi Olympic dau tien dien ra o dau?*
</questions>

---

<!-- chunk: answer_3 -->
<teacher_script pause="0">
Sau day la dap an. Day la nhung kien thuc chung rat thu vi ve the thao.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Dap an:**
1. There are usually 22 players (11 on each side).
2. It normally lasts 90 minutes (divided into two halves).
3. They take place every four years.
4. A marathon is 42,195 kilometres long (26 miles and 385 yards).
5. They took place in Olympia (in Ancient Greece) (in 776 BC).
</answer>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Cau 1: Co bao nhieu nguoi choi trong mot tran dau bong da? Dap an la <eng>There are usually 22 players (11 on each side)</eng> - Thuong thi co 22 cau thu moi ben 11 nguoi. Gom co thu mon, hau ve, tien ve, tien dao. Neu co cau thu bi the do thi so nguoi se giam xuong nha.
</teacher_script>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Cau 2: Mot tran bong da keo dai bao lau? Dap an la <eng>It normally lasts 90 minutes (divided into two halves)</eng> - Thuong keo dai 90 phut duoc chia thanh hai hiep. Giua 2 hiep co thoi gian nghi khoang 15 phut. Neu la tran dau loai truc tiep ma hoa thi co the co hiep phu 30 phut hoac da luan luu 11m.
</teacher_script>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Cau 3: The van hoi Olympic thuong dien ra bao lau mot lan? Dap an la <eng>They take place every four years</eng> - Chung dien ra moi bon nam mot lan. Olympic la su kien the thao lon nhat the gioi. Co Olympic mua he va Olympic mua dong, moi loai cung 4 nam mot lan.
</teacher_script>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Cau 4: Mot cuoc chay marathon dai bao nhieu? Dap an la <eng>A marathon is 42,195 kilometres long (26 miles and 385 yards)</eng> - Mot cuoc chay marathon dai 42.195 km. Con so nay xuat phat tu khoang cach tu thanh pho Marathon den Athens o Hy Lap co dai. Theo truyen thuyet, mot nguoi linh chay doan duong nay de bao tin chien thang.
</teacher_script>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Cau 5: The van hoi Olympic dau tien dien ra o dau? Dap an la <eng>They took place in Olympia (in Ancient Greece) (in 776 BC)</eng> - Chung dien ra o Olympia - Hy Lap co dai nam 776 truoc Cong nguyen. Day la Olympic co dai. Con Olympic hien dai dau tien duoc to chuc nam 1896 tai Athens, Hy Lap. Ten goi <eng>Olympic</eng> xuat phat tu dia danh <eng>Olympia</eng> nha.
</teacher_script>

<explanation>
**Giai thich chi tiet:**

| # | Cau hoi | Dap an | Giai thich |
|---|---------|--------|------------|
| 1 | How many players? | **There are usually 22 players (11 on each side).** | Thuong co 22 cau thu moi ben 11 nguoi |
| 2 | How long? | **It normally lasts 90 minutes (divided into two halves).** | Thuong keo dai 90 phut chia thanh hai hiep |
| 3 | How often? | **They take place every four years.** | Chung dien ra moi bon nam mot lan |
| 4 | How long is marathon? | **A marathon is 42,195 kilometres long (26 miles and 385 yards).** | Mot cuoc chay marathon dai 42.195 km |
| 5 | Where first Olympic? | **They took place in Olympia (in Ancient Greece) (in 776 BC).** | Chung dien ra o Olympia - Hy Lap co dai nam 776 truoc CN |
</explanation>

### Bai 4 trang 21 - Interview your partners

<teacher_script pause="0">
Bai 4 <eng>Work in groups</eng> nha. Lam theo nhom, phong van ban ve the thao di.
</teacher_script>

<task>
**De:** Work in groups. Interview your partners using the following questions. You may ask for more information.
**Dich de:** Lam viec theo nhom. Phong van cac ban cua em su dung cac cau hoi ben duoi. Em co the hoi nhieu thong tin hon.
</task>

<questions type="speaking">
**1.** What sports / games do you play in your free time? Do you play them well? When and how often do you play them?

*Ban choi mon the thao / tro choi nao trong thoi gian ranh? Ban co choi chung tot khong? Ban choi chung khi nao va bao lau mot lan?*

**2.** Which sport / game do you like watching on TV?

*Ban thich xem mon the thao / tro choi nao tren TV?*

**3.** Did you join any clubs?

*Ban da tham gia cau lac bo nao chua?*

**4.** If you don't play sport, what do you often do in your spare time?

*Neu ban khong choi the thao, ban thuong lam gi vao thoi gian ranh roi?*
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="0">
**Report your interviews to the class.** - Bao cao cac bai phong van voi ca lop nha.
</teacher_script>

<dialogue>
**Mau tra loi:**

| English | Vietnamese |
|---------|------------|
| **A:** What sports / games do you play in your free time? | **A:** Ban choi mon the thao / tro choi nao trong thoi gian ranh? |
| **B:** In my free time I play badminton and chess. | **B:** Trong thoi gian ranh, minh choi cau long va co vua. |
| **A:** Do you play them well? | **A:** Ban choi chung tot khong? |
| **B:** I'm quite good at badminton, but I'm not good at chess. | **B:** Minh choi cau long kha gioi, nhung minh khong gioi co vua. |
| **A:** When and how often do you play them? | **A:** Ban choi chung khi nao va bao lau mot lan? |
| **B:** I play badminton at the weekend and I play chess every evening before bedtime. | **B:** Minh choi cau long vao cuoi tuan va minh choi co vao moi buoi toi truoc khi di ngu. |
| **A:** Which sport / game do you like watching on TV? | **A:** Ban thich xem mon the thao / tro choi nao tren TV? |
| **B:** I like watching football matches on TV. The players are really amazing. | **B:** Minh thich xem cac tran dau bong da tren TV. Cac cau thu thuc su tuyet voi. |
| **A:** Did you join any clubs? | **A:** Ban co tham gia bat ki cau lac bo nao khong? |
| **B:** Yes, I did. I'm a member of the chess club of my school. | **B:** Minh co. Minh la thanh vien cua cau lac bo co vua cua truong. |
| **A:** Do you often do anything else in your spare time? | **A:** Trong thoi gian ranh ban con lam gi khac nua khong? |
| **B:** Well, I also collect plastic bottles to recycle and make new things in my house. | **B:** A, minh cung thu gom chai nhua de tai che va lam cac do dung moi trong nha. |
</dialogue>
