<questions type="fill_blanks">
**1.** Duong looks _______. He often does karate.

*Duong trong _______. Cau ay thuong tap karate.*

**2.** Mai likes coming to the _______. The equipment there is great.

*Mai thich den _______. Thiet bi o do rat tuyet.*

**3.** Duong played _______ with Duy yesterday, and he won.

*Hom qua Duong choi _______ voi Duy, va cau ay da thang.*

**4.** Mai and Duong will meet at Superfit _______ on Sunday.

*Mai va Duong se gap nhau tai Superfit _______ vao Chu nhat.*

**5.** Mai will _______ to Superfit Club.

*Mai se _______ den Superfit Club.*
</questions>

<!-- chunk: answer_2 -->
<teacher_script pause="0">
Da het thoi gian. Sau day la dap an dung.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Dap an:** 1. fit | 2. gym | 3. table tennis | 4. Club | 5. cycle
</answer>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Cau 1 dien <eng>fit</eng>. Trong hoi thoai, Mai noi voi Duong: <eng>You look fit!</eng> - Ban trong khoe manh qua! Vay la Duong trong khoe manh nha. <eng>Look fit</eng> nghia la trong khoe, trong cuong trang. Nguoi tap the thao thuong xuyen se look fit.
</teacher_script>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Cau 2 dien <eng>gym</eng>. Ngay cau dau tien cua hoi thoai, Duong noi: <eng>Wow! This gym is big!</eng> - Cha! Phong tap nay to qua! Vay la hai ban dang o phong tap gym. Mai thich den gym vi thiet bi o do rat tot nha.
</teacher_script>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Cau 3 dien <eng>table tennis</eng>. Duong noi: <eng>I play table tennis. Yesterday I played with Duy, and I won!</eng> - Minh choi bong ban. Hom qua minh choi voi Duy va minh da thang! Ro rang luon, Duong choi bong ban voi Duy va thang nua.
</teacher_script>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Cau 4 dien <eng>Club</eng>. Duong noi dia chi cau lac bo: <eng>It's Superfit Club, in Pham Hung Road</eng>. Ten day du la Superfit Club - Cau lac bo Superfit. Dung quen viet hoa chu C nha vi day la ten rieng.
</teacher_script>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Cau 5 dien <eng>cycle</eng>. Duong noi: <eng>You can cycle there</eng> - Ban co the dap xe den do. <eng>Cycle</eng> la dong tu, nghia la dap xe. Meo nho: <eng>cycle</eng> (dong tu) = dap xe, <eng>cycling</eng> (danh tu) = mon dap xe. Vi du: I cycle to school - Toi dap xe den truong. I like cycling - Toi thich mon dap xe.
</teacher_script>

<explanation>
**Giai thich:**

| # | Cau | Dap an | Tim trong hoi thoai |
|---|-----|--------|---------------------|
| 1 | Duong looks ___. | **fit** | Mai noi: "You look fit!" |
| 2 | Mai likes coming to the ___. | **gym** | Duong noi: "This gym is big!" |
| 3 | Duong played ___ with Duy. | **table tennis** | Duong noi: "I play table tennis" |
| 4 | ...at Superfit ___. | **Club** | Duong noi: "It's Superfit Club" |
| 5 | Mai will ___ to Superfit. | **cycle** | Duong noi: "You can cycle there" |

**Phan biet:**
- **cycle** (dong tu) = dap xe
- **cycling** (danh tu) = mon dap xe
</explanation>

### Bai 4 trang 17 - Work in pairs

<teacher_script pause="0">
Bai 4 <eng>Work in pairs</eng> nha. Lam theo cap, hoi dap xem minh co thich the thao khong di.
</teacher_script>

<task>
**De:** Work in pairs. Ask your partners these questions to find out how sporty they are.
**Dich de:** Lam viec theo cap. Hoi ban nhung cau hoi nay de tim hieu muc do yeu thich the thao cua ban.
**Yeu cau:** Tra loi cac cau hoi. Neu da so la A -> ban yeu the thao. Da so B -> can choi the thao nhieu hon!
</task>

<questions type="speaking">
**1.** Can you swim? *(Ban co biet boi khong?)*

- A. Yes. *(Co.)*
- B. No. *(Khong.)*

**2.** Do you play outdoors every day? *(Ban co choi ngoai troi moi ngay khong?)*

- A. Yes. *(Co.)*
- B. No. *(Khong.)*

**3.** Do you usually do morning exercise? *(Ban co thuong tap the duc buoi sang khong?)*

- A. Yes. *(Co.)*
- B. No. *(Khong.)*

**4.** What do you usually do at break time at school? *(Ban thuong lam gi vao gio giai lao o truong?)*

- A. Play in the schoolyard. *(Choi trong san truong.)*
- B. Sit in the classroom. *(Ngoi trong lop hoc.)*

**5.** What do you think of sports and games? *(Ban nghi gi ve the thao va tro choi?)*

- A. Very good. *(Rat tot.)*
- B. Not useful. *(Khong huu ich.)*

**If your answers to the questions are mostly "A", you are sporty. If they are mostly "B", do more sport and try to be more active.**

*Neu cau tra loi cua em hau het la A, em rat yeu the thao. Neu cau tra loi cua em hau het la B, em nen choi the thao va co gang nang dong hon.*
</questions>

## Phan biet DO / PLAY / GO + sports

| Dong tu | Dung voi | Vi du |
|---------|----------|-------|
| **DO** | Mon vo, yoga, aerobics | do karate, do judo, do aerobics |

| **GO** | Mon co -ing | go swimming, go cycling, go fishing |
</grammar>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Meo nho: <eng>DO</eng> dung cho nhung mon tap ca nhan nhu vo, yoga. <eng>PLAY</eng> dung cho nhung mon co tinh dong doi, doi khang hoac dung bong, ban co. <eng>GO</eng> dung cho nhung mon co duoi -ing ma phai di chuyen.
