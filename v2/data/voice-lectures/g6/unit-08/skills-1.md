### Bài 5 trang 22 - Talk about a sportsperson

<teacher_script pause="0">
Chúng ta cùng làm Bài 5 <eng>Talk about a sportsperson</eng>. Các em hãy chọn một trong hai vận động viên ở bài 4 và nói về người đó.
</teacher_script>

<task>
**Đề:** Choose one sportsperson in 4. Talk about him/her. Use the following cues.
**Dịch đề:** Chọn một vận động viên ở bài 4. Nói về anh ấy/cô ấy.
**Gợi ý:** His/Her name, The sport he/she plays, Why he/she is famous, You like him/her or not and why.
</task>

<grammar>
## Cấu trúc nói về vận động viên:

| Nội dung | Cấu trúc |
|----------|----------|
| Tên | His/Her name is... |
| Năm sinh | He/She was born in [year] in [country]. |
| Môn thể thao | He/She plays [sport]. |
| Tại sao nổi tiếng | He/She is famous for... |
| Thành tích | In [year], he/she won/became... |
| Ý kiến của em | I like/admire him/her because... |
</grammar>

<dialogue>
**Bài mẫu - Hoàng Giang:**

His name is Hoàng Giang. He was born in 1978 in Việt Nam. He plays shooting. He is famous for being No.1 sportsman in shooting. In 1996, he took part in a shooting competition. In 2001, he won a gold medal for shooting. I like him very much because he's talented.

**Bài mẫu - Jenny Green:**

Her name is Jenny Green. She was born in 1972 in Greenland. She plays golf. She is famous for being one of the best female golfers in history. In 1987, she became a member of local golf club. In 1994, she took part in a female golf tournament. In 2002, she became the female golf champion. I admire her a lot because she is really skillful and healthy.
</dialogue>
