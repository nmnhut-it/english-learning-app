<grammar>
## Future Simple (Thì Tương lai đơn)

### Cách dùng:
Dùng để nói về hành động sẽ xảy ra trong tương lai.

### Công thức:

**Khẳng định (+):**
| Chủ ngữ | will | Động từ nguyên mẫu |
|---------|------|-------------------|
| I/We/You/They/He/She/It | will | V |

Ví dụ: I **will** go to school tomorrow. *(Tôi sẽ đi học ngày mai.)*

**Phủ định (-):**
| Chủ ngữ | will not | Động từ nguyên mẫu |
|---------|----------|-------------------|
| I/We/You/They/He/She/It | will not (won't) | V |

Ví dụ: I **won't** go to school tomorrow. *(Tôi sẽ không đi học ngày mai.)*

**Nghi vấn (?):**
| Will | Chủ ngữ | Động từ nguyên mẫu |
|------|---------|-------------------|
| Will | I/we/you/they/he/she/it | V? |

Ví dụ: **Will** you go to school tomorrow? *(Bạn sẽ đi học ngày mai chứ?)*

Trả lời ngắn: Yes, I will. / No, I won't.

### Dấu hiệu nhận biết:
- tomorrow (ngày mai)
- next week/month/year (tuần/tháng/năm tới)
- in the future (trong tương lai)
- soon (sớm)
- tonight (tối nay)
- later (sau này)

### Mẹo nhớ:
- **WILL** = SẼ (tương lai)
- **WON'T** = SẼ KHÔNG (phủ định tương lai)
- will + V nguyên mẫu (không chia, không thêm s/es/ed gì hết!)
</grammar>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Nhớ nha: <eng>will</eng> là SẼ, <eng>won't</eng> là SẼ KHÔNG. Sau <eng>will</eng> và <eng>won't</eng> luôn dùng động từ nguyên mẫu, không chia gì hết á. Ví dụ: <eng>will go</eng>, <eng>won't come</eng>, <eng>will have</eng>. Ok bây giờ làm bài tập nha.
</teacher_script>

### Bai 2 trang 41 - Complete the conversation

<teacher_script pause="0">
Bai 2. <eng>Complete the conversation with will or won't</eng>. Hoan thanh cuoc hoi thoai voi <eng>will</eng> hoac <eng>won't</eng>. Lam 1 phut nha.
</teacher_script>

<task>
**De:** Complete the conversation with *will ('ll)* or *won't*.

**Dich de:** Hoan thanh cuoc tro chuyen voi *will ('ll)* hoac *won't*.
</task>

<questions type="fill_blanks">
**A:** Oh, no. The dog ran away again!

**B:** Don't worry - he (1) _______ come back.

**A:** Are you sure he (2) _______?

**B:** OK, he might not come back today. But I'm sure he (3) _______ come back tomorrow.

**A:** I don't believe you! He (4) _______ come back. We (5) _______ never see him again. I'm sure.

**B:** Oh, look ... Here he is!
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="60">
Ok 1 phut lam bai nha.
</teacher_script>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Dap an ne.

Cho trong 1: <eng>will</eng> - <eng>Don't worry, he will come back</eng>. Dung lo, no se quay lai. B dang tran an A nen dung <eng>will</eng> de khang dinh.

Cho trong 2: <eng>will</eng> - <eng>Are you sure he will?</eng>. Ban co chac no se lam vay khong? Day la cau hoi xac nhan.

Cho trong 3: <eng>will</eng> - <eng>I'm sure he will come back tomorrow</eng>. Toi chac chan no se quay ve vao ngay mai. B van tin tuong nen dung <eng>will</eng>.

Cho trong 4: <eng>won't</eng> - <eng>He won't come back</eng>. No se khong quay lai. A phu nhan, khong tin nen dung <eng>won't</eng>.

Cho trong 5: <eng>will</eng> - <eng>We will never see him again</eng>. Chung ta se khong bao gio gap lai no nua. Chu y: <eng>will never</eng> = se khong bao gio, khac voi <eng>won't</eng>.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Dap an:** 1. will ('ll) | 2. will ('ll) | 3. will ('ll) | 4. won't | 5. will ('ll)

**A:** Oh, no. The dog ran away again!
*(Oi, khong. Con cho lai bo di roi!)*

**B:** Don't worry - he (1) **will** come back.
*(Dung lo lang - no se quay lai.)*

**A:** Are you sure he (2) **will**?
*(Ban co chac no se lam nhu vay khong?)*

**B:** OK, he might not come back today. But I'm sure he (3) **will** come back tomorrow.
*(Duoc roi, hom nay no co the se khong quay ve. Nhung toi chac chan no se quay ve vao ngay mai.)*

**A:** I don't believe you! He (4) **won't** come back. We (5) **will** never see him again. I'm sure.
*(Toi khong tin! No se khong quay lai. Chung ta se khong bao gio gap lai no nua. Toi chac chan.)*

**B:** Oh, look ... Here he is!
*(O, nhin kia ... No day roi!)*
</answer>

<!-- chunk: might-theory -->
<teacher_script pause="0">
Truoc khi lam Bai 4, hoc them ve <eng>might</eng> nha. <eng>Might</eng> cung la tro dong tu chi tuong lai, nhung no co nghia la CO THE, dien ta kha nang co the xay ra nhung chua chac chan. <eng>Will</eng> thi chac chan hon, con <eng>might</eng> thi chua chac.
</teacher_script>

<grammar>
## Might (Co the)

**Might** dung de dien ta dieu gi do co the xay ra trong tuong lai nhung chua chac chan.

**Cong thuc:**
- Khang dinh: S + might + V (nguyen mau)
- Phu dinh: S + might not + V (nguyen mau)

**Vi du:**
- We **might** go on holiday to the Moon. *(Chung ta co the di nghi tren Mat trang.)*
- They **might not** talk to us. *(Ho co the khong noi chuyen voi chung ta.)*

**So sanh:**
- **will** = se (chac chan hon)
- **might** = co the (chua chac chan)
</grammar>

<translation>
**Tam dich:**

**Trong tuong lai** *(Henry, 11 tuoi)*

Chung ta co the di nghi tren Mat trang.
Chung ta co the o do trong mot thoi gian dai.
Chung ta co the co mot thoi gian tuyet voi.

<questions type="true_false">
**1.** Jenny thinks we might live with robots.
*(Jenny nghi rang chung ta co the song voi robot.)*

**2.** Henry thinks we might travel to the Moon.
*(Henry nghi rang chung ta co the du hanh den Mat trang.)*

**3.** Jenny thinks robots might not clean our houses.
*(Jenny cho rang robot co the khong don dep nha cua cua chung ta.)*

**4.** Henry thinks we will stay on the Moon for a short time.
*(Henry nghi rang chung ta se o tren Mat trang trong mot thoi gian ngan.)*

**5.** Jenny thinks robots might help us to do the housework.
*(Jenny nghi rang robot co the giup chung ta lam viec nha.)*

**6.** Henry thinks we might not have a great time on the Moon.
*(Henry nghi rang chung ta co the khong co khoang thoi gian tuyet voi tren Mat trang.)*
</questions>

<teacher_script pause="90">
Ok doc bai tho va lam bai. 1 phut ruoi nha.
</teacher_script>

<teacher_script pause="0">
Dap an ne. Giai thich tung cau:

Cau 1: TRUE - <eng>Jenny thinks we might live with robots</eng>. Dung, Jenny viet <eng>We might live with robots</eng> trong bai tho cua co ay.

Cau 2: TRUE - <eng>Henry thinks we might travel to the Moon</eng>. Dung, Henry viet <eng>We might go on holiday to the Moon</eng>.

Cau 3: FALSE - <eng>Jenny thinks robots might not clean our houses</eng>. Sai! Jenny viet <eng>They might clean our houses</eng>, tuc la robot CO THE don dep, khong phai KHONG don dep.

Cau 4: FALSE - <eng>Henry thinks we will stay on the Moon for a short time</eng>. Sai! Henry viet <eng>We might stay there for a long time</eng>, tuc la thoi gian DAI, khong phai ngan.

Cau 5: TRUE - <eng>Jenny thinks robots might help us to do the housework</eng>. Dung, Jenny viet robot se don dep va giat quan ao, tuc la lam viec nha.

Cau 6: FALSE - <eng>Henry thinks we might not have a great time on the Moon</eng>. Sai! Henry viet <eng>We might have a great time</eng>, tuc la CO THE co thoi gian tuyet voi, khong phai KHONG co.
</teacher_script>

<answer>
**Dap an:** 1. T | 2. T | 3. F | 4. F | 5. T | 6. F

1. **T** - Jenny nghi chung ta co the song voi robot. (Dung - Jenny viet "We might live with robots")
2. **T** - Henry nghi chung ta co the du hanh den Mat trang. (Dung - Henry viet "We might go on holiday to the Moon")
3. **F** - Jenny cho rang robot co the khong don dep nha cua. (Sai - Jenny viet "They might clean our houses" = CO THE don dep)
4. **F** - Henry nghi se o tren Mat trang trong thoi gian ngan. (Sai - Henry viet "a long time" = thoi gian dai)
5. **T** - Jenny nghi robot co the giup lam viec nha. (Dung - Jenny viet robot don dep, giat quan ao)
6. **F** - Henry nghi co the khong co thoi gian tuyet voi. (Sai - Henry viet "We might have a great time")
</answer>
