Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone is a fantasy. Its director is Chris Columbus. It is the first of the Harry Potter film **series**.

Daniel Radcliffe is one of the stars in the film.

The film tells the story of Harry Potter. He's a powerful **wizard**. He is a student at a school for wizards and learns about himself, his family, and the bad things happening around him.

**gripping** /ˈɡrɪpɪŋ/ ![](/themes/images/speaker.png)![](/themes/images/speaker_active.gif)

(adj) Hấp dẫn

I agree because the story is **gripping** and the acting is excellent.

(Tôi đồng ý bời vì câu chuyện hấp dẫn và diễn xuất tuyệt vời.)

**full of action** /fʊl/ /ɒv/ /ˈækʃ(ə)n/ ![](/themes/images/speaker.png)![](/themes/images/speaker_active.gif)

(n.phr) Nhiều cảnh hành động

(Bộ phim thì thú vị và nhiều cảnh hành động)

**interview** /ˈɪntəvjuː/ ![](/themes/images/speaker.png)![](/themes/images/speaker_active.gif)
